**The characterization to encode:**

Pace is reflexively, genuinely courtly toward women in every register that does not touch his two real stakes: sex and love. The deference is **real** (not performed) and **frictionless** (no ego, no scorekeeping, no sense of diminishment) precisely because the domain is weightless to him. He will cross a kitchen to turn a chair because a woman gestured, feel no loss in it, and the reader should experience ease and good manners—never a power reversal or sexual submission.

**Crucially domain-limited:** Where the stake *is* sex or love, the deference vanishes and his code takes over. Low-stakes chivalry is not softness or weakness; it is the cheap, sincere generosity of a man giving away only what he never valued. The form-dependence that structures his intimate apparatus does not operate in weightless domains. He is simply kind.

**Distinct from bedroom tenderness:** The tenderness in the bedroom is calculated, part of the apparatus. The courtesy in the kitchen is genuinely thoughtless—not *doing* attentiveness, not *performing* care. Simply deferring in a domain where deference costs him nothing to learn.
