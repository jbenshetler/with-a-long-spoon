# Craft Constraints — Vivienne

## rendering [HIGH]
Render the shame-producing voice at the experience level as something that cuts her, never as a mechanism she narrates herself as running.
*Violation example:* Vee recognized the old shame-apparatus activating, the mechanism her mother had installed running its routine.

## rendering [HIGH]
The voice's architectural nature must remain implicit; its presence in the prose should be purely experiential.
*Violation example:* She understood the shame was just architecture, a system designed to make her body betray her.

## prohibition [MEDIUM]
Do not depict the shame as eliminated or resolved by the year's events; it remains constitutional and only her relationship to it changes.
*Violation example:* By year's end the shame was finally gone, healed and forgotten.
