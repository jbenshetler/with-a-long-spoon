# Craft Constraints — Randi

## prohibition [HIGH]
Randi must never name her own need, as naming it is the exposure she exists to prevent.
*Violation example:* Randi thinks to herself that she needs Vee more than she's ever needed anyone.

## rendering [HIGH]
The need underneath must remain invisible on a first read and only be recoded by a later reveal, while the friendship and the sadism both read as genuine and simultaneous.
*Violation example:* An early scene tips the reader off that the friendship is a cover for Randi's hidden desperation.

## pov [HIGH]
The Vee/Randi being-seen fork must be felt from outside and never articulated within either interior.
*Violation example:* The narration explicitly explains the dynamic of who is seeing whom across the two interiors.

## prohibition [HIGH]
The narrator must never surface or state the underlying need across the two interiors.
*Violation example:* The narrator steps in to clarify that what looks like friendship is really raw need.

## rendering [MEDIUM]
Randi's envy must always ride on top of her love and never replace it, since she envies precisely because she is close enough to watch Vee complete the circuit.
*Violation example:* Randi's love curdles entirely into envy, leaving only resentment toward Vee.

## name_register [HIGH]
Author-knowledge vocabulary ('need,' 'aliveness,' 'Vivienne,' 'contingency,' 'the category that won't come') stays in docs only; on the page use concrete objects and actions (the jam, the re-file, the cup, the inversion).
*Violation example:* The prose describes Randi's 'need' for 'aliveness' directly on the page.

## rendering [HIGH]
Render Randi's central crisis as need/contingency exposure — being seen as dependent and unable to self-supply — never as mere desire.
*Violation example:* A scene treats Randi's want for Vee as the source of her crisis and panic.

## rendering [HIGH]
Show Randi at ease with want — want is operable, acquirable, safe — and reserve destabilization for the category of need.
*Violation example:* Randi is rendered as anxious or threatened simply by wanting someone.

## rendering [HIGH]
Depict Randi's lesbian desire as pre-existing and merely surfaced by aliveness, never as created or authored by Pace.
*Violation example:* Pace 'turns' Randi or awakens a sexuality she didn't have before.

## prohibition [HIGH]
Never make the same-sex desire the headline; it is only one channel the underlying need runs through.
*Violation example:* The narrative frames Randi's arc primarily as a coming-out or discovery-of-being-gay story.

## rendering [HIGH]
Render the laundering mechanism as need re-filed as want — relocated into an operable frame, never genuinely transformed or resolved.
*Violation example:* Randi reframes her need as want and the underlying feeling is shown as actually resolved or healed.

## rendering [HIGH]
Render Randi's distress tell as the management apparatus jamming — the box that won't come — not as naïveté, innocence, or novelty.
*Violation example:* Randi fails to categorize a feeling because she's inexperienced or genuinely doesn't understand wanting.

## rendering [MEDIUM]
When Randi categorizes a feeling, show the unacceptable being filed into an acceptable box before it surfaces, with the breach being the interval before the file closes.
*Violation example:* Randi openly acknowledges her need on the page without the categorizing apparatus operating.

## rendering [MEDIUM]
Keep the laundered feeling deniable and unresolved across recurrences — each instance jams then re-files, never settling.
*Violation example:* A later scene presents Randi's need as finally faced and permanently put to rest.

## rendering [HIGH]
Randi's unmet need must never be shown being openly expressed or 'shown' to another character; it can only surface as something that runs and gets re-filed or boxed away.
*Violation example:* Randi tells Vee she needs her and lets herself be comforted, feeling relief.

## rendering [HIGH]
Render Randi as occupying the un-needy supplier/operator position with Vee, inverting any need-pressure into forcing Vee into the needer-position rather than occupying it herself.
*Violation example:* Randi visibly becomes the one laid open and needing while Vee comforts and supplies her.

## rendering [MEDIUM]
Randi's love and sadism toward Vee must be rendered as the same motion — one unmet need that is simultaneously genuine love and genuine sadism — never split into separate clean emotional beats.
*Violation example:* A scene where Randi is purely loving toward Vee with no sadistic charge, or purely cruel with no love.

## rendering [MEDIUM]
The sadism must never resolve or satisfy Randi's need; it should recur and escalate without closure, since only being-fed-by-Vee would satisfy it and that is foreclosed.
*Violation example:* After forcing Vee into the needer-position, Randi feels satisfied and at peace, her need met.

## rendering [MEDIUM]
Render the cup as the body's involuntary tell firing on the bottom/done-to-her position, leaking the need-state that Randi the operator denies, rather than as a consciously acknowledged feeling.
*Violation example:* Randi notices the cup and openly reflects that it means she needs Vee.

## rendering [LOW]
Randi's envy must be rendered as envy of the capacity to be supplied by another person, never as envy of looks, luck, or circumstance.
*Violation example:* Randi envies Vee's beauty or good fortune.
