# Condensed — Grain #2 — Worn in Plain Sight

*Vignette-thread · Unwritten · recurring, March onward · seen only in Vee-POV scenes*

Randi wears Vee's scrunchies around Vee — and Vee is **thrilled**: the glamour queen is mimicking *her* style.

## Mechanism — evidence in the open, wearing a perfect disguise

The sighting is fully conscious and completely misread. Vee's reading is flattery — Randi liked hers enough to buy the same — and it is exactly what her generous-priored, unguarded apparatus would reach for. This is the **loving-Randi cover**, twin to grain #3's loving-Pace cover ("he must have refilled them for me"): every corrupted datum in the campaign gets explained by love. The evidence isn't suppressed; it's metabolized as intimacy — which actively protects Randi at grain #3, because "Randi wears scrunchies like mine" is already filed under *friendship* and can't combine with the form arithmetic into "Randi has *my* scrunchies."

## Shape

- **First instance — spoken.** Vee delighted (*"did I actually convert you?"*); Randi's double-register reply: *"You've ruined me. I used to have standards."* Warm, frank, fun on first read; on reread she is being complimented on wearing the theft to its owner's face, and accepting the compliment. No word changes.
- **Recurrences — unremarked.** Set dressing riding scenes that do other work; no camera linger, no authorial wink. Randi's curated discipline (one accent, never a scheme) makes an off-register scrunchie *almost* visible — and the mimicry explanation absorbs exactly that visibility.
- **Late variation — post-Restoration.** One sighting where the delight takes a half-second longer to arrive. The only trace that her instrument is still running underneath. Theme-and-variations inside the grain.

## Reader irony

The reader carries the First Flicker, so the jolt is available (*wait — are those—?*) while Vee beams — but it stays unprovable: scrunchies aren't unique, and Randi adopting a Vee-ism is genuinely plausible. The warmth of the scenes seduces the reader into half-buying the flattery too; the *is-Vee-paranoid* question extends to the reader: *am I?*

## The reciprocity cut

Everything in this friendship flows Randi→Vee — the shoes, the borrowed witty lines, the steering. The scrunchies appear to flow Vee→Randi: the only mutuality Vee ever perceives in the friendship, the moment she believes she gives Randi something too. On reread, the only thing that ever flowed from Vee to Randi was stolen.
