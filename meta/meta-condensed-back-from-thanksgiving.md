## Summary

Vee returns and tells Cassie about the break. A cropped induction photo is the physical evidence that surfaces her father's question and forces the telling: she can pour out the *external* problem — how do you make Pace legible to her father — at length, but still can't say what he is to her. The dodge relocates from the surface to the register that actually hurts; Cassie clocks the closed door. The telling earns the Christmas Fight without prejudicing Pace.

- The relocated dodge: she opens the dress/father axis because it's safe, but the deeper question — what is he to you — stays the closed door. *"Both. Probably both."* holds for the register that costs. Cassie respects it; Vee grateful, and a little, not.

Guard: the monologue's antagonist is her father's frame, never Pace — he is the thing that won't fit, not a man failing her.
