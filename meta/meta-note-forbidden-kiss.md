## What this scene is

The Refusal, on the page: the first gate where the taste-taboo is named and the door slams — *"that's gross / I'm not gay."* It opens the spring transgressive through-line (the Leak runs live and conscious from here) and it's the line First Taste later crosses. Vee POV, close third (his read stays unrendered).

## The mechanism — the un-clocked kiss

He goes down on her; he comes up to kiss her still coated; **he doesn't hide it and doesn't announce it.** She's coming down from the orgasm, not tracking — she doesn't register what's on his mouth until their lips meet and she feels the slick. *Then* the recoil. The not-clocking is the gate's hinge: she never got the beat to refuse beforehand.

## The imperfection — render it as a grain, not a crack (load-bearing tone guard)

This is the one place the scene touches the book's thesis, and it must be handled **inside the taste thread's governing rule** (`meta-note-taste-thread.md`): *warm and sexy on both reads, no sinister signal ever — the enabling genuinely is enabling.*

- On **first read** it is *nothing* — a man kisses her after going down on her, she's grossed out, *ugh, no.* The smallest of busts, the kind most people would shrug off and forget.
- On **reread** what surfaces is **depth, not dread**: that he wanted her past this, that the whole thing was always pointed somewhere. Never *he violated her.* The faint aimed-ness — his care contracting to the bare, deniable minimum because this taboo is the gate he most needs down — is the **first grain of the year's accretion** of the consent-hole (the maneuver-not-force gap his architecture can't see), not a visible breach. The hole becomes visible by *accumulation* and at the reckoning, never by a flagrant early beat (`meta-arch-pace.md`, `meta-thesis.md`).
- **If it reads as Pace-the-manipulator, it's overcooked — cut the signal until it's just a kiss she didn't want.** The fuller, designated imperfection beat is **First Taste's inducement** (`meta-note-taste-thread.md`); don't let this out-loud it.

## Body before mind

The recoil is physical first — head turning, lips sealing, the back of the hand — and the words ride on top, small and reflexive. Keep *"that's gross / I'm not gay"* exactly that size. The conflation underneath (female wetness in her mouth = gay = wrong) is the real barrier and stays **subterranean** — the line states it; the prose never analyzes it.

## Pace's response

He **files it and doesn't push** — wipes his face, they go on. Render his instant acceptance of the no as **genuine care** first (it is). His not-pushing is the same chivalric grammar working honestly; the deniable aimed-ness lives only in the reread, never in a visible dodge or sulk.

## The post-coital install (the engine)

Her on her side, holding him while he sleeps, reaching back over the wiped kisses where a trace still got through. This single retrospect does two jobs:

1. **Retro-installs the Leak.** It telescopes the whole Oct→Jan history of his incomplete wiping into one beat — so **First Night and every scene since stay clean**, never carrying a rendered taste-plant (which is the point: we don't touch First Night). The retrospective-sweep technique the thread already sanctions.
2. **Lights the conscious wondering** — *what is it, what am I missing* — minor key, under the affection, never disgust-obsession. After tonight the Leak is **conscious**; it runs live as gracenotes through the spring scenes.

## The wipe becomes a known boundary

The casual, incomplete wipe predates this (subliminal). Forbidden Kiss is where it becomes a **deliberate, mutually-known** protocol — he wipes now *because* she drew the line — and where the Leak crosses into her awareness.
