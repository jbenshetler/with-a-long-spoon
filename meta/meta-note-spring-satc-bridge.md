## Committed clothing-loan thread

Randi's clothing loans are now a committed spring SATC device, extending the CW-Dance shoe loan and resolving at the threesome shoes.

Randi is short and slender; Vee is tall and lush. Clothes that are conservative on Randi become borderline-scandalous on Vee. This gives the thread its double register:

- First read: glamorous friend shares designer pieces and luxury fabrics Vee cannot afford but has looked at in fashion magazines. Vee feels chosen, sophisticated, close to Randi, and unwilling to disappoint her.
- Reread: Randi authors Vee's surface, increases public exposure, and gives Vee a built-in alibi for display: she did not choose the outfit; she borrowed it.

The escalation should move from offer to tease to assumption to insistence to selection-without-asking. Use direct insistence sparingly; one "I won't hear of it" is charming, repeated pressure tips too early.

Rules:

- Loans, not gifts. No money moves before the Dressing Room. The Dressing Room remains the first deployment of Randi's money against Vee.
- The loans begin with shoes after the CW-Dance Debrief apology. On Her Floor (late Jan) introduces the first clothing loan (a stretchy-knit, Newton-ring-iridescent skirt for a sorority dance party), establishing the thread before spring expansion. The loan is motivated by Vee's class wound: Randi invites her as plus-one to a world Vee wanted but couldn't afford to pledge, and Vee has no right dress — so the borrowed skirt is belonging-on-loan, the evening's governing rhyme. Brooke recurs in this scene as the face of the social penalty, pricing Randi (not Vee) by warmth.
- Loan-tending stays deniable until the Dressing Room. Adjusting, zipping, smoothing, and clasping sit in the same gray register as goodbye kisses and fitting-hands. No overt sexual hand/tending touch before the nipple tweak; On Her Floor's public open-mouth kiss is a narrow kiss-axis exception because Harrison/the show-frame preserves deniability and keeps the dressing-room touch threshold intact.
- The clothes must generate fresh shame: public display, body-as-betrayer, intention betrayed by figure. Randi can truthfully say a skirt is not that short or a dress does not need a bra because on Randi, that is true.
- The clothing thread also carries Vee's fascination with Randi's body in safe aesthetic registers: fabric, fit, lipstick, eyebrows, jewelry, scent, shoes, polish, cost, and composure.

Final object spine:

- First rung: Randi loans shoes for Vee's CW-Dance apology to Pace.
- Spring: Randi increasingly dresses Vee for brunch and, occasionally, for Pace-adjacent contexts.
- Threesome night: Vee wears Randi's shoes to the event. Randi knows the night; Vee does not.
- Ending: after the reveal, Vee refuses to put Randi's shoes back on and leaves barefoot. Randi's shoes remain at Pace's house with the panties.

## Vee reads Randi - spring planting discipline

Vee's fascination with Randi's body should remain barely sublimated into style. She does not think "I want Randi." She notices precision, expense, polish, control, and self-construction.

Safe registers:

- Lipstick: edge, color, confidence, the mouth made deliberate.
- Eyebrows / cat eye: skill, symmetry, command of the face.
- Jewelry: wrists, throat, hands, small flashes that can be read as taste before body.
- Scent: nameable intimacy; the safe question that later makes the reveal-night scent-swap work.
- Clothes: body displaced into fabric, fit, cut, cost, and Randi's taste.
- Shoes: closeness without fit-problems — clothes travel between them too (lent, they transform on Vee), but the shoe travels *cleanly*, no distortion, which is what carries it into the reveal.

The full body-read should not arrive early. Spring should show Vee working harder to keep the attention in aesthetic categories until the Sheer Dress / Dressing Room format-break makes that laundering begin to fail.

## Placement decisions

- **The First Love** is never SATC-retold.
- **Jitterbug/Jar** does not spend a bathroom masturbation rung. If there is physical overwhelm, keep it tenderness-register and non-completion.
- **On Top** should preferably land before Restrained because active frustration cleanly precedes surrendered rest. This is a preference, not yet a hard chronology lock. See `meta-note-on-top.md`.
- **First Taste** precedes the effective threesome bridge. The Pointing-Game variation and agreement come after Vee has crossed the self-taste threshold.
- **Pace does not supply or select a stranger for Vee.** Pace and Randi have a pre-existing understanding from the pointing-game/arrangement. From Vee's position, the woman remains unknown until the event; in reality, the first time Vee meets the woman in the arrangement is during the threesome.
- **Scrunchies are locked at reveal.** Randi's hair is wild and barely contained by Vee's green-and-white polka dot scrunchies.
