# Condensed — The Ending Sequence

*Scene · Architecture complete; prose not yet drafted · immediately after {{The Threesome and Reveal}}*

Vee leaves with full knowledge, full anger, full ownership of her transformation. She **integrates rather than breaks down** — a half-minute of not breathing, cold reconstruction — frees herself, removes her own blindfold, and leaves looking at the **objects, not the people:** the green sheets on the bed, the two pairs of shoes side by side, Danaë on the wall (all seen and left, `meta-note-danae.md`); the green panties stay unseen in his drawer. She closes the bedroom door and walks out **barefoot to her Corolla.** **The friendship betrayal hits hardest.** Pace reads the wrong betrayal (the lesbian element; never computes the engineered friendship). Randi is hollow, known at last, unable to feel her loss to its floor — the muffling she chose in his bathroom mirror on the night of the Bench returned as sentence. Probably without explaining (to Pace and Randi; she tells Cassie after — the thesis scene, `meta-arch-vivienne.md` → The Cassie Scene).

**Vee does not put Randi's shoes back on.** She walks home barefoot — wordless, deliberate, the first purely-her choice made in full knowledge. Throughout the year her body announced its wanting without her permission; this is her body doing something she decided. The shoes were Randi's **Mary-Jane peep-toes**, the ones Randi specifically chose and lent when Vee confided there was a big sexy event coming (possibly named the threesome outright, given the SATC closeness). Randi called them her *fuck-me pumps* when she loaned them — the term load-bearing because Randi literally fucks Vee that night (narration keeps the object accurate; *fuck-me pumps* stays in Randi's mouth). And they are one of **two** borrowed pairs at the exit: Randi wears her own **Roman heels** (the pair she loaned Vee for {{Nothing Underneath}}), and the two pairs sit side by side — Vee realizes she wore **both** borrowed pairs to Pace's house; **the shoes don't distinguish the two women.** Randi has worn hers to Pace herself; she knows exactly which ones she was lending. Randi's shoes stay at Pace's house alongside the panties. Two artifacts, two women, neither coming back. The reader who has tracked the shoe loans all year — first loan at the {{Broken In}} apology, recurring across the year each time Vee was going to see Pace, escalating to this — feels the thread close here.
