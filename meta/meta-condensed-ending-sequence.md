# Condensed — The Ending Sequence

*Scene · Architecture complete; prose not yet drafted · immediately after The Threesome and Reveal*

Vee leaves with full knowledge, full anger, full ownership of her transformation — probably without explaining. Pace reads the wrong betrayal (the lesbian element; never computes the engineered friendship). Randi is hollow, known at last, unable to feel her loss to its floor — the muffling she chose in his bathroom mirror on the night of the Bench returned as sentence. The panties stay in his house — not a promise, an artifact.

**Vee does not put Randi's shoes back on.** She walks home barefoot — wordless, deliberate, the first purely-her choice made in full knowledge. Throughout the year her body announced its wanting without her permission; this is her body doing something she decided. The shoes were Randi's — the ones Randi specifically chose and lent when Vee confided there was a big sexy event coming (possibly named the threesome outright, given the SATC closeness). Randi called them what they were: *fuck-me pumps.* Warm, conspiratorial, girlfriend-loaning-girlfriend-her-best-shoes. Randi has worn them to Pace herself; she knows exactly which ones she was lending. Randi's shoes stay at Pace's house alongside the panties. Two artifacts, two women, neither coming back. The reader who has tracked the shoe loans all year — first loan at the CW-Dance Debrief apology, recurring across the year each time Vee was going to see Pace, escalating to this — feels the thread close here.
