# Notes — Sorority

*Companion to the drafted scene (`scenes/sorority.md`) and its condensed (`meta-condensed-sorority.md`). Holds the craft intent deliberately not on the page. For the surface beats, read the condensed.*

---

## The title

**Sorority** names the relational frame this scene actually builds — the confidante bond crowned by Vee's closing thought that Randi is the best friend she's ever had. The literal referent is off-page on purpose: Randi is a sorority officer whose pre-Pace/Vee life centered on it, and she keeps privileging Vee over her sisters (already once at the smoothie shop, again later). At a shallow first read the word is a light **misdirect** — sisterhood, girl-talk — and the false-sisterhood charge is a buried third layer that needs the novel's machinery to surface, so it never winks. Etymology (soror = sister) does the quiet work; no reader has to reach for it.

## The "buckled" seed — carry it in the prose, not the title

We deliberately kept restraint/yield off the marquee (a title headlining her collapse would over-seed and dent earn-the-dark). Instead, **lightly seed the BDSM ideas symbolically here**, a year ahead of where they go literal (the blindfold/threesome). The register is domestic and deniable; nothing is named. Carriers already latent in the draft, to lean into on revision:

- **The buckle itself.** The boutique shoe is canon a **four-inch strap** (Induction canon) — so a buckle fastening is consistent; render it as *fastened/secured*, the ordinary fact of a strap shoe doing the quiet double of being *strapped in*.
- **Held, not balanced.** The arch "firm as a hand under her foot," her weight *caught and carried* rather than balanced — restraint as steadiness, the good kind of being held.
- **Her buckling.** The somatic yield at Randi's thumb across the frosted-plum nail — the wobble, the hand shooting to Randi's shoulder — and her resolve **buckling** on the credit-card leap. Let the word *buckle* do both jobs (fastened / gave way).

Diction to favor on revision: *buckle · fasten · strap · held · gave way.* Keep it symbolic and deniable — the surface stays friendship and a beautiful dress; the restraint reading is reread-only, resonance never exposition.

## Reserve

**Buckled** was the runner-up title and stays on the shelf for the **first literally-restrained threshold** — it lands harder where the restraint is real than here where it's only figured. Don't spend it on this step.
