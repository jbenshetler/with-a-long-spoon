# Note — The Practice Room

*Companion to the unwritten scene: beat structure and unrendered craft. The container, the after-not-before structure, the bathroom-progression placement (and its supersession of the SATC-doc rung), and the plant ledger live in [[meta-condensed-practice-room]] and are not restated here.*

**POV:** close third on Vee throughout. Randi stays opaque — her reading of Vee is shown only as Vee noticing she has been looked at, never from inside Randi. **Heat-by-kind:** Movement I opens dry-wry (the stats lecture) then turns warm/playful (earns the light); Movement II psychologically intense; Movements IV–V cold-under-warm. **Master guard:** first pass must read as *the glamorous friend delightedly drags the juicy details out of me, then sees me out warm* — every dark reading reread-only.

## Movement I — stats, the first peel, and the funny version

Engine: **Vee drives, Randi supplies the venue.** Vee's urgency to tell is the motor (deniable for Randi); Randi furnishing the sealed room is the instrument (invisible as such). **This is the scene where the motor becomes visible in the blocking:** the first time Vee peels Randi off, not the reverse.

- **The stats open (the outliers mirror, silent).** Cassie is present, as at every stats class; the three sit two-thirds back in the tiered room, Vee in the middle, the professor at the front doing his ritual (scrolling slides forward and back). Render him to canon — unnamed, flat/deadpan ("tire-pressure label" register), lays the concept out and lets it sit. Topic: **outliers, additive vs. innovational** — a shock that hits one point and washes out (you lift it out, the line goes on) versus a shock that enters through the part that carries forward and is in everything after, decaying but never gone. **Silent, never explained** (Global Craft Rule): Vee catches the open, copies a definition, sinks back under into the weekend and into him, connects nothing. Fresh concept; prior mirrors are latent-variables, Simpson's, continuous-vs-discrete (reserved March). *Vary the wording:* the Simpson's scene owns "underslept and overfull"; don't echo it.
- **The cover-thread plants here.** Vee arrives not having slept since the weekend, full of him; she lays her own distraction at Pace's feet. This false self-explanation runs the length of the scene and is spent at the hug (Movement V).
- **The first peel (the reversal).** Up to now Randi has been the one peeling Vee out, away from Cassie. **This is the first time Vee peels Randi off** — she maneuvers to get Randi alone after class, leaving Cassie behind, routing the swat to the one who can receive it (the Cassie/Randi sort). The who-leads migration (owned by [[meta-plan-satc-tracks]]) made literal in the staging. Surface: Vee is just eager to share the juicy thing with her fun friend. Reread: the target now initiates the isolation, seeks her own handler. Do not underline.
- **To the room.** Off-campus is blocked (Randi has a sorority commitment after, no time to leave campus). The default being unavailable is what strips the production format — the bare room is what is left, not a thing Randi staged. *Unspoken ghost:* since Dear they minimize on-campus meals; never explained, manifests only as the easy option not being on the table.
- **The makeout lore is Vee's, nostalgic.** Randi suggests the practice rooms (sealed, close, empty midday). *Vee* supplies the freshman-year-makeout history and colors at her own association — self-implication, body before mind. Keep it nostalgic lore, never an invitation; one flicker in Vee, written off. The **door closing** (soundproofing sealing them in) is the real opening image.
- **Time pressure is a feature.** The compressed clock pre-licenses "quick" later (the bathroom, the rushed everything) and sets up the hug asymmetry (Randi has to go; Vee is slow to let go).
- **Motive stack (all genuinely warm):** return the loaned shoes + report to the coach + trade a sexy story. Because Randi coached the reconciliation (courage, the shoes), she is *owed the ending* — the extraction needs no manufacturing; it is the mentor collecting on her own coaching. Warmth real AND instrumental, never resolved; the coaching was good and it helped. Render Randi's interest as proud-coach, never leering.
- **Opener (supersedes the condensed's "Your turn"):** *"Okay. The shoes. Tell me everything."* — the taking-turns ritual and the coaching claim in one breath.
- **Vee is innocent of her own motive.** She walks in to tell the *funny* story, not knowing she carries the charge. The room and Randi do the converting.
- **The asymmetric telling is the tell.** Vee narrates the *romance* full and proud — borrowed heels, the height inversion, Pace's gallant save (the "loan" line) — then **skates the swat** as a cute aside (*and then he swatted me, it was so dumb, I laughed*). The asymmetry (minutes of romance, four words of swat) is what Randi reads. Mechanism is **savor, not interrogate:** Randi simply will not let the skated part stay skated.

## Movement II — the extraction

Extraction-first: **Vee's body arrives at the answer unled; Randi supplies the word.** The warming is Randi's questions, not a preceding story.

- **"Being a brat"** — Vee's own word, innocent (sassy/mouthy); carries the scene-meaning (provoke→punish) she does not know. **Unexplained plant**, keep single, do not gloss — it pays at We Find Out.
- **"He was tickling me, so I swatted him"** — Vee frames it as an equal game, retaliation, agency.
- **"A game of slap-and-tickle?"** — Randi's superior-knowledge beat: the worldly phrase, relished, collusive not condescending (pulls Vee up beside her, both laughing). Both-tests: cute/risqué first read; renaming innocent play as foreplay on reread.
- **"So — did you deserve it?"** — the hinge from laughter into narrowing. Appetite-reframe wearing a joke (did you *want* it). **Vee's deflection** — *"God, probably / I was being awful"* — agrees she was a brat while staying in the register of mischief: she accepts the reframe without knowing it, and it **detonates retroactively** a beat later when her body answers yes.
- **Narrow to the one swat.** Out of the whole game, the one *he* gave back. The **wet hand** (he had turned from the sink) is the connoisseur's detail — innocent to ask about, lethal at re-summoning the body. Vee re-approaches; her body re-fires on its own.
- **The instant (locked):** *"It went right through me. And I'm sure he saw it."* Holds both halves — the involuntary event (*went right through me*) and the exposure (*I'm sure he saw it*). Charge sits on the **exposure, not the sensation**. She gets herself there; do not let her explain why his seeing it matters.
- **Then/now, Randi WORDLESS.** He saw it then; Randi sees it now, in the retelling. Randi keeps her line on the *past* (the *"Mm. I bet he did"* register) and affirms the exposure was the good part; the **present-tense seeing lives entirely in Vee's POV** (Vee feels the eyes, colors harder). Naming present arousal from Randi's mouth tips her predatory-visible — do not.
- **Nipples re-fire NOW (interior).** Deliberate rhyme with the kitchen tell, now triggered by *recall alone, in company, fully clothed* — the scene's thesis (the mechanism now runs on memory). Same vocabulary, one riser higher: the **wetness** is the escalation past the kitchen, and it forces the bathroom. *Experience, cannot voice.*
- **Oblique "have you ever—" that dies.** Saved oblique on purpose (directness is reserved for spring); the obliqueness measures how far she has been pulled. The unfinished question is the door that hands off to Randi's story.

## Movement III — Randi's story (the mouse that roars)

After the extraction, as amplifier + installer, pointing at We Find Out. **Camera stays on Vee's reception** — the explicit anecdote is the stimulus; Vee's interior (color, nipples firing on words alone, the welcome-horror of hearing herself) is the literature.

- **Intercut, never a block.** Break the story across Vee's tells so it never becomes a monologue and "very welcome" lands *on* her, not at a paragraph's end: the **count** → she colors; **"his hand would slip"** → the nipples re-fire; **"very welcome"** → the wetness that tips her to the bathroom.
- **The man is un-Pace by texture, not by mandate.** Slight, thin, soft-spoken, *overlookable* in public — the mouse. The distinguisher is that this is **meek/overlookable quiet**, NOT Pace's *warm-magnetic* public self (the room leans toward Pace; nobody clocks the mouse). His dominance is **theatrical-loud in private** (over the knee, skirt up, count out loud, "like a naughty little girl") — the loud-fake register that keeps Vee from connecting it to Pace's quiet-real. Do not force every Randi decoy to be Pace's photographic negative; uniform anti-Pace decoys calcify and telegraph.
- **Counting is safe** — not a Pace fingerprint anywhere in canon; keep it.
- **The hand-slip is a deliberate installer-rhyme with We Find Out, safe only because the register is opposite.** Keep Randi's version **roguish** (*his hand would slip… very welcome* — oops/naughty/it-felt-good). **Never proof.** Pace's move at We Find Out is clinical/diagnostic (*"We have to check if you like it," "You set this up,"* the glistening finger as accurate evidence — see the bible's best-phrasings). If the mouse *names* or *shows* her arousal as proof, the decoy steals Pace's signature and the later subversion dies. Same touch, opposite meaning; the mismatch is the protection.
- **Installers to keep:** *"It was humiliating. But it did make me buck."* (shame→arousal, the involuntary "buck") and *"By then? Welcome. Very welcome."* (warm-first principle, pointing at We Find Out). **"Very welcome" is the trigger** — it tips Vee's lit body into the wetness she must go manage. Land it and cut; do not let Randi keep talking past it.
- **Randi narrates from the sub position** — disarming candor (frank-register as social proof) that models the position she is steering Vee toward. The orchestrator performing her own delicious submission is a quiet irony; do not underline it.
- Agreed register sketch: *"Oh, baby. Sometimes it's the mouse that roars. Mine — you'd never have picked him out of a room. Slight little thing, soft-spoken, apologized to the furniture. But get him home, and if I'd had too much wine and gotten a little flirty with other men? I'd hear about it when we were alone. Over his knee, like a naughty little girl. Skirt up, panties down, and I had to count. Out loud. Every one, while he turned me as red as he liked. Humiliating. And God, it made me buck. And always, toward the end, his hand would slip. Mm-hm. Right between. I know how it sounds — it would've been awful if I weren't already warm. By then? Welcome. Very welcome."*

## Movement IV — the bathroom and the close

- **Rung one of the bathroom-trip progression: quick suppression** (the progression and its supersession of the SATC-doc rung are in the condensed). **Engine: she refuses to *consent* to her own want.** The unbearable part is not the arousal, it is owning it; to touch herself would convert *something happening to her* into *something she chose*. Suppression preserves the fiction that none of it is hers — she keeps it "without consent" because consenting is the line she cannot cross. The novel's load-bearing theme turned inward.
- **Self-imposed, not obedience.** Predates the Telling (chronology Flag 19a) — nobody told her not to; she polices herself. This is the baseline the rule later corrupts.
- **Rendering rules:** gesture, not deliberation (her hand moves before she does; she catches it; no decision narrated). Sensation, not anatomy. **Short on the page** — quick suppression is quick prose; later rungs earn length, this one earns meaning by being the one she gets out of fast. **Cap, do not resolve** — the arousal goes under the composure and runs live into the return; defuse it in the stall and the close has nothing to land on.
- **Fresh indignity:** her body answered a *story* — not his hand, not even her own memory. New floor; it is why the bathroom is needed this time.
- **Silent outlier payoff.** This is where the stats-open lands, unstated: she treats the response as an *additive* outlier (lift it out, it never happened); it is *innovational* (already in the system, carrying forward). Never connect it on the page.
- Agreed sketch: *"The latch was loud in the little room. She sat down without meaning to. Her body was still going under the thin fluorescent light, the story still running in it, and the proof of that was already there before she wanted to know it, soaked through, made by nothing but a woman talking. Her hand knew the way. It moved before she did. She caught it and put it flat against her own knee and held it there. Not here. Not over a story. To do that would be to say yes to it, and she would not say yes to it. She ran the cold tap and pressed her wrists under it and looked at the woman in the mirror, flushed to the throat, too bright in the eyes, not collected at all, and she stayed only until the woman could pass for collected. She never did settle whether Randi had seen. There was no way to go back and look."* Calibration left open: keep the one engine-line (*to do that would be to say yes to it…*) or cut to pure gesture and let the hand on the knee carry it wordless.
- **The close (supersedes "I hope everything is alright"): *"All better?"*** Plain words + an **arch look Vee cannot verify** + the **trap of a question** (every answer is wrong). The cleverer the line the less deniable, so keep the words innocent and let **Vee's guilt supply the knife** — "better" is the one loaded word, retrievable on reread; delivered the instant she sits it clocks the timing for free (*all better, already?*). **The warm move-on is the devastation:** Randi lets it go, gathers her bag, never has to have meant anything; Vee is left holding a question that now cannot be asked. Rung one of the deniable-naming staircase (event → *"All better?"* → *"Sticky fingers?"* at the We Find Out debrief) — keep it **soft** to preserve runway.

## Movement V — the hug riser

The physical capstone: **the suppressed charge leaks out through a permitted form.** A hug is innocent, allowed, "only what they do," so the wanting she just denied finds the one channel that requires no admission. She gets the contact without owning the want — the bathroom engine, one room later.

- **Escalate the hold, not the kiss.** The ritual is unchanged in structure — the hug, the mouth, the doubled second press, *"See you soon, gorgeous"* (the staircase state is owned by [[meta-plan-satc-tracks]]). **Render it in fresh words each scene** — recognize the kiss by its shape, never by repeated phrasing (no recurring "soft and unhurried / the small second press"). **The only riser is the hold:** Vee is the last to let go.
- **Randi does NOT extend it.** She steps back to normal distance, easy, already half-turned to where she has to be. If Randi lingers she tips predatory; the **asymmetry is the entire riser** (she clocks it; we do not show that — opaque).
- **Warm, never heated** — no anatomical charge or it telegraphs; the dangerous thing is only that Vee does not want it to end (reads as emotional need, with a bodily undertow the reader catches and Vee does not).
- **Undercurrent now toward Randi**, *past faint, not named* — Vee's body holds onto Randi; she notices late and writes it off (the cover-thread from the stats open: hadn't slept, still full of him). The clock sharpens the exposure (Randi has to go).
- **Runway:** take only the hold-increment here. Save the loaded held-eye-contact (the writing-off visibly wearing thin) for the next physical step.
- **The button (decided): Pace-misattribution.** She writes the not-letting-go off to the cover-thread from the stats open — the not-sleeping, the still-full-of-him. The reader, who watched Randi produce every response, supplies the correction. Sketch: *"…She noticed it the way you notice a held note after the song has moved past it, and she let go. She'd hardly slept; she was still so full of him, of the whole weekend. She put it down to that."* ("Long morning" was cut — it did not ring true; the misattribution to Pace is the writing-off doing real work.)

## Scene-specific guards

- **Sustain one unbroken thread of not-knowing** from the nipples beat → bathroom → *"All better?"* → hug. Vee never learns what Randi saw; that unresolved question is what makes *"Sticky fingers?"* collectible later.
- **Keep Randi opaque throughout** — every read of Vee shown only as Vee-noticing-she-is-looked-at. At most the bathroom timing is filed, never narrated from inside Randi.
- **Earn the dark by being light:** the slap-and-tickle stretch is genuinely funny and warm; the cold is pattern-only.
- **Do not telegraph the mouse=Pace inoculation.** The decoy must read as a fun spicy anecdote, full stop.
- **The stats mirror stays silent.** Outliers (additive vs. innovational) is taught and copied, never connected to Vee on the page; the payoff is the bathroom, unstated.
- **Do not underline the reversal.** The first-time-Vee-peels-Randi beat reads as eagerness on the surface; its meaning is reread-only.
- Heat varies by kind across the scene (dry → playful → intense → cold-under-warm), never an intensity arms race.
