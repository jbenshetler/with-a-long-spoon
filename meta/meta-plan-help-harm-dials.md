# Help/Harm Dials — The Fourth-Category Inventory

*Companion to `novel_thesis.md`. Working tool for the prose. Inventory of operations in the novel where a single act, configuration, or behavior produces help and harm simultaneously — the fourth-category structure named in the thesis. Each entry names the operation, the parties it acts on, the goods it produces, the harms it produces, the temporal vantage from which each becomes legible, and craft notes about how the duality should be rendered in scene.*

*Purpose: protect the prose against rendering any of these operations as one-sidedly helping or one-sidedly harming. The fourth-category claim only works if the scenes render both faces. This document is the inventory of dials the prose must keep set to "both."*

---

## How to Use This Document

Each entry follows a standard format:

- **Operation:** what happens in the scene/configuration
- **Helps whom, how, when:** the goods produced, the party receiving them, the temporal vantage at which they are received
- **Harms whom, how, when:** the harms produced, the party receiving them, the temporal vantage at which they are received (often retrospective)
- **Avoidability:** whether the carrier had honorable options (harm-two structure) or whether the harm is constitutive of the appetite's operation (harm-one structure) — note that both are in the fourth category
- **Craft notes:** how the prose should render the duality; warnings about which collapse to avoid

When a scene draft is being checked, run it against the relevant entries. If the scene renders only one face of the operation, the scene has collapsed the fourth-category structure into one of the prescriptive frameworks the novel transcends.

When new operations are discovered in the writing, add them here. The list is generative, not closed.

## Pace-on-Vee Operations

### The Secretions Reframe (First Night)

**Operation.** Pace, in their first sexual encounter, greets Vee's abundant lubrication with delight rather than treating it as evidence to be politely managed past. *Welcomes the wetness.* Beams at her body's involuntary responses where she has been condemned for them by her own internalized voice for years.

**Helps Vee in real time.** Opens a register her shame had been suppressing for years. Begins the transformation of shame-as-suppressor into shame-as-fuel. Inaugurates the new relationship to her own arousal that the year will deepen. *She is, by every measure available to her, more alive than she has been.* The shame-fuel arousal wiring fires for the first time in a register she can occupy without flight. This is the meal arriving.

**Harms Vee retrospectively.** Installs the appetite's specifications. *Before the first night, the hunger was diffuse and ambient. After the first night, the hunger has specifications and the specifications cannot be met by what is available to her afterward.* The first night did not slowly accumulate the harm; it installed the substrate-reshaping in a single sustained operation. The cracker-and-meal architecture begins here. *The hunger she will carry for the rest of her life was installed the first night.*

**Avoidability.** Harm-one register. Pace cannot operate at less than his appetite's required depth without not feeding the appetite at all. The harm is constitutive of the appetite's operation on a substrate like Vee.

**Craft notes.** The first night must read as the apex of Vee's life-to-date in real time. Do not let the prose foreshadow the cost. The reader on second read will see the substrate-reshaping; the reader on first read sees the meal arriving. *The retrospective harm-legibility is the novel's structural device;* do not undermine it by signaling in the moment.

### The Fitting (Russet Dress Construction)

**Operation.** Pace makes Vee a russet silk dress for her honor-society induction, matched to her coloring rather than to the magazine reference she described. He builds the dress on her body, measuring with deliberate restraint. The secretions reframe operates wordlessly when she dreads the wet spot and opens her eyes to find him already beaming up at her face, never having looked down.

**Helps Vee in real time.** Demonstrates seeing-her at a resolution she has never experienced. Establishes the deeds-not-words declaration of love-through-craft. Tips her from nervous to bold. Confirms that the secretions reframe is durable across scenes, not just first-night novelty. *She experiences this as evidence that what is happening between them is real and unusual.*

**Harms Vee retrospectively.** Deepens the attachment-at-resolution that the configurations available after the year will not be able to engage. *The fine man will not make a dress.* The fine man will not match her coloring instead of the reference. *Every future relationship will be measured against this and found wanting.* The deepening of the deeds-language increases the specific cost of its eventual absence.

**Avoidability.** Harm-one register. The making-of-the-dress is constitutive of Pace's chivalric grammar operating; he cannot turn it off without not being himself.

**Craft notes.** The scene is already drafted. The retrospective cost should not be signaled in the scene; it is in the architecture, available to the reader on reread.

### The Bondage Scene

**Operation.** Pace ties Vee, removing the question of her hands during sexual operations.

**Helps Vee in real time.** Removes the question of involuntary-response shame by removing the question of what her body would do voluntarily. Gives her a register of sexual freedom her shame-wiring could not produce otherwise. The trust-deepening operation that the year requires.

**Harms Vee retrospectively.** Like the fitting, deepens the depth of attachment and the depth of trust at registers no future partner will reach without first earning the right to reach them, which will take time and may never occur. *Trust-deepening operations are loss-deepening operations.*

**Avoidability.** Harm-one register.

**Craft notes.** Render the trust as the trust-deepening. Do not let the prose tip toward danger. The reader who feels the danger in real time is reading the scene wrong; the danger is architectural, not phenomenological.

### Pace's Listening at Domestic Moments

**Operation.** Pace listens to Vee at the resolution he listens at. Remembers what she tells him. Makes her things without invoicing. Configures his attention around her in ways men in her life have not.

**Helps Vee in real time, continuously.** This is what makes the relationship the relationship Vee experiences it as. Across hundreds of small moments, his attention is at a resolution Vee has not been at the receiving end of before. *The whole year is partly this.*

**Harms Vee retrospectively.** Establishes a baseline she will measure all subsequent attention against. The fine man's listening will read as inattention by comparison. *She cannot un-know what attention at this resolution feels like.*

**Avoidability.** Harm-one. Pace cannot listen at less than his characteristic resolution; the resolution is the apparatus operating.

**Craft notes.** Sprinkled across the year in small beats. Render each as the warm small fact it is. The accumulation is what produces the retrospective cost; no single beat should announce itself as load-bearing.

## Pace-on-Randi Operations

### The Bench Scene

**Operation.** Pace administers attentive spanking braided with sustained edging and emotional care, reaching Randi at a depth no prior partner has reached. *She has the most intense sexual experience of her life to that point.* She begs at the bench.

**Helps Randi in real time.** The most intense sexual experience of her life. *In real time, this is the apex.* Her body confirms the operation as it unfolds. She is more alive than she has been in twenty-one years of curated surface.

**Harms Randi immediately.** Installs the hunger her apparatus has no equipment to satisfy. Produces vulnerability her apparatus cannot integrate. The regret surfaces within hours of the bench. *The bench wound is substantially done in the single sustained scene.* This is harm-one at its fastest installation — not cumulative across years but installed in one operation.

**Avoidability.** Harm-one register. Pace's operation was honoring every consent protocol his architecture could see. He did not have access to the question that would have surfaced what he was about to install.

**Craft notes.** The bench scene is the worked example of harm-one fast-installation. The scene should render Randi at her apex in real time, with no flinch toward the regret that will surface afterward. The reveal of the wound's installation happens off-stage, in Randi's bathroom moment, and is concealed from Pace. *In the bench scene itself, the operation is the operation;* the harm becomes legible in the next scene, in her body, alone.

### Pace's Continuing Operation on Vee (As Experienced by Randi Through Vee's Reports)

**Operation.** Pace continues to operate his appetite on Vee across the year. Vee, in good faith, reports to Randi about Pace at the SATC lunches.

**Helps Randi in real time** (in a sense). The SATC lunches operate in part on what Vee delivers. Randi's appetite, channeled through the operation on Vee, is fed by what Vee brings. *Without Vee's reports, the SATC scenes have less material to operate on.* The configuration's continued aliveness in Randi depends partly on Vee's continued delivery of Pace-material.

**Harms Randi continuously.** Every report is fresh evidence that Pace's seeing was real and that the inadequacy at the bench was Randi's. *The bench wound is updated weekly across the year.* Randi receives the harm in private; she conceals the harm from Vee; the harm compounds.

**Avoidability.** Harm-one register with respect to Pace (he has no access to what his continued operation on Vee is doing to Randi because Randi has concealed the wound from him). The configuration's structure produces this; Pace is not running an additional operation on Randi here.

**Craft notes.** Each SATC lunch where Vee speaks about Pace operates on this dial. Render Vee's warmth in delivering the reports as her real warmth; render Randi's face as managing-something-she-will-not-show; do not let either party register what is happening in the room as harm-compounding. The reader on reread sees the mechanism.

## Randi-on-Vee Operations

### The SATC Scene Amplifications

**Operation.** Randi, across the year of lunches, amplifies Vee's humiliation around her own bodily responses. *Operates as the dominant in the BDSM register the lunches develop into.* Engineered confessions, conversational pressure, small humiliations under cover of warmth.

**Helps Vee in real time.** Fires the shame-as-fuel wiring in the register Pace's reframe cannot reach. *Pace short-circuits the humiliation step; Randi amplifies it.* Both are required for the year's full transformation of Vee's relationship to her own shame. The SATC scenes produce arousal-at-depth in a register Vee's wiring had been hungering for. The friendship-intimacy at depth Vee experiences as the most intense friendship of her life. *These pleasures are real.*

**Harms Vee retrospectively.** Operates on her at a depth that becomes part of the configuration she walks out of at the reveal. The reframe-operation Randi performed is real and partly hers to carry; the friendship that was the operation's surface is what the reveal destroys. *The shame-as-fuel wiring continues to require an engager;* the engager available to Vee post-year will not engage at this amplitude in this register. *Vee will be the woman whose wiring was fired by Randi specifically* and will not have access to that firing afterward.

**Avoidability.** Configuration-dependent. Randi could not have operated this way on Vee without being the friend Vee had at the depth Randi was; the depth was constitutive of the operation. Harm-one structure with respect to Randi's appetite. *And* the operation was running inside the larger configuration Pace and Randi engineered, which has harm-two avoidability.

**Craft notes.** Render the SATC scenes as the high-intensity BDSM scenes they are *and* as the genuinely close lunches Vee experiences them as. Both. Vee's warmth toward Randi in these scenes is real. Randi's pleasure in the operations is real. The reader on reread sees the multi-register operation; the reader on first read may see only the friendship.

### The Engineered Specific Friendship-Texture

**Operation.** Randi makes herself unusually available to Vee — sharper, more attentive, more engineered-around-Vee than an unconfigured friend would be. *The friendship's specific texture is partly the product of Randi running operations on Vee.*

**Helps Vee in real time, across the year.** The friendship is the best of her life. Randi's attentiveness is sharpened by the configuration's purpose; this delivers to Vee felt-experience of being-known at a depth she would not have received from an unconfigured friend. *Vee's experience of Randi as the best friend of her life is genuine and partly produced by the configuration's operation.*

**Harms Vee retrospectively.** Same configuration that delivered the friendship is the configuration whose reveal destroys it. *The depth of the friendship is the depth of the loss.* The reveal contaminates retrospectively what the friendship was when it was operating.

**Avoidability.** Harm-two register. Randi could have refused to participate in the configuration; she could have disclosed at any point; she chose not to because the configuration was the only configuration in which she was alive.

**Craft notes.** The friendship's specific intensity is doing structural work; the prose must render the intensity at full force. *Vee was not naive to value the friendship at the depth she valued it;* the friendship was, at that depth, real. The reveal does not retroactively erase what the friendship delivered in real time.

### Randi Operating on Pace's Effect on Vee (The Cross-Channel Operation)

**Operation.** Randi receives Vee's reports about Pace at the SATC lunches and uses them as material for the operations on Vee. *Vee's description of Pace's attentiveness becomes the substrate Randi humiliates her around.* Vee's confidences are turned, in real time, into the levers Randi uses.

**Helps Vee in a complex register.** The lunches feel intensely intimate to Vee partly because Randi is using Vee's confidences with such precision. *The precision feels like being-known.* Vee experiences this as the friendship operating at its depth.

**Harms Vee in a different complex register.** Vee is being operated on through her own disclosures. Her trust is the lever. Her good-faith sharing is the material the operation uses. *The harm is in the structure;* Vee does not experience it as harm in real time because the operation is rendered as warmth and attention.

**Avoidability.** Harm-two register. Randi knows she is doing this; could choose not to; chooses to because the operations are her aliveness.

**Craft notes.** The most uncomfortable register of the SATC scenes. Render the intimacy of Vee's confidences as real; render Randi's use of them as her real apparatus operating; let the reader feel the dial without naming it. *Vee never names this.* The reader sees it across multiple lunches.

## Configuration-Wide Operations

### The Threesome Itself

**Operation.** The threesome occurs. Vee, Pace, and Randi in the room together. Randi kisses Vee. Vee tastes their combined fluids on Randi's mouth. *The mechanism of the reveal triggers in this kiss.*

**Helps Vee in real time** (up to the kiss). The most intense sex of her life. The apex of the year's intensities converging. Multi-party operation Vee has been brought toward across the year by a configuration that has been preparing her to receive it.

**Helps Pace in real time.** The apex of his appetite's full-pitch operation. Every channel of his attention online. The year's preparation converging.

**Helps Randi in real time** (mixed). The fulfillment of the configuration's purpose. *The kiss is the closest physical-operational analog Randi has to her year-long admission of need.* The operation she has been hungering for is operating.

**Harms Vee at the kiss and after.** The reveal is what the kiss triggers. *Vee's reading of the kiss reorganizes her understanding of the year.* The configuration's information-architecture collapses. The catastrophe begins in this moment.

**Harms Pace at the kiss and after.** *He does not perceive the harm at the kiss.* His apparatus does not see what the kiss reveals to Vee. The harm to Pace is the year's catastrophe and the slow-attenuation future; he experiences only its leading edge at the kiss as a not-yet-understood wrongness.

**Harms Randi at the kiss and after.** The kiss is her apex; the morning is her loss-of-the-configuration; the rest of her life is the seeking. *The kiss is what she has been working toward and what ends the only configuration in which she has been alive.*

**Avoidability.** The threesome is a harm-two event for Pace (he organized this; he could have refused; honorable exits were available). The threesome is harm-one for Randi at the operational level (her appetite required the operation). For Vee, the threesome is the configuration's culmination; she has been brought to it by both her own brave consent and the configuration's careful preparation.

**Craft notes.** The threesome scene is the maximum-engagement event for Pace and the maximum-amplitude event for the whole configuration. *The kiss is the load-bearing sole channel of revelation.* Up to the kiss, the scene runs as the apex of intensity. The kiss is the pivot. After the kiss, Vee's interior reorganizes. Render the apex at full intensity; do not let the prose anticipate the reveal; let the kiss be the device.

### The SATC Lunches as Configuration Operation

**Operation.** Weekly. Vee tells Randi about Pace. Randi tells Vee about her life (sanitized). The friendship operates at depth. The configuration runs.

**Helps Vee continuously.** Best friendship of her life; depth-of-confidence she has not experienced before; intensity-of-being-known.

**Helps Randi continuously.** Six-channel aliveness; the only configuration in which she is alive; the operations on Vee delivering aliveness she has no other access to.

**Helps Pace by proxy.** The lunches sustain the configuration; sustaining the configuration sustains Pace's access to the substrate; he believes the lunches are warm friendship between the women, which is what he wants the configuration to look like from outside.

**Harms Vee continuously.** Operating on her at depth she does not know is operation. Her confidences are being used. The trust is the lever.

**Harms Randi continuously.** Bench wound updated weekly. *Vee's reports compound Randi's wound; Randi conceals the compounding from Vee; the wound accumulates inside her.*

**Harms Pace by proxy.** The configuration's continuation deepens his appetite's operation on Vee, deepens the substrate-reshaping, accumulates the year's transformation. *He thinks he is just having his year with Vee.* He is also accumulating costs to his future.

**Avoidability.** Configuration-wide; harm-two structure with respect to the disclosure-not-made; harm-one structure with respect to each appetite operating at its required depth.

**Craft notes.** Each lunch operates this dial. Some lunches surface the operation more than others; some are quieter. Render the warmth at every lunch; render Randi's small concealments; render Vee's small unease at things she cannot quite name (Cassie's warning, the writing-off effort, the bathroom trips). The pattern is what the reader sees on reread.

*Craft notes.* Render Pace's experience of the configuration as warm. *Do not let the prose suggest he sees through his own apparatus.* The apparatus is delivering the warm perception; the warm perception is closing the integration; the closing is what the catastrophe operates against.

### The Year of Aliveness Itself

**Operation.** Vee lives the year with Pace and Randi. Months pass. The configuration deepens. She is, by every measure available to her, having the most intense year of her life.

**Helps Vee continuously.** *The year is the best of her life.* She would not have wanted to not have it. Even after the reveal, even with the catastrophe — *the trade was correct on the terms available the first time.* The thesis insists on this. The novel insists on this. *Vee got something real.*

**Harms Vee permanently.** The hunger she walks out with. The configurations available afterward that cannot feed what the year fed. The retuning of the instrument. The no the year taught her. *The cost is permanent.* And it is the cost of having had what she had.

**Avoidability.** The year as a whole is the configuration's operation; harm-two for Pace (he could have disclosed at any point and stopped the year); harm-one for Vee's appetite (the depth at which her hunger was being fed was the depth at which the cost was accumulating); harm-one and harm-two together for Randi (the configuration was her aliveness; she could have refused but did not).

**Craft notes.** This is the master dial of the novel. Every scene operates somewhere on it. *The year must be worth it for Vee.* The reader must close the book believing this. The cost must also be real. *Both required.* If the prose tips toward the year-was-not-worth-it reading, the novel collapses. If the prose tips toward the cost-is-not-permanent reading, the novel also collapses.
