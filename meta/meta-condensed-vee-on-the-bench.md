## Content

The same apparatus, the opposite outcome. Pace praises her lushness — the body in the space the bench was built to leave empty, breasts hanging free where Randi's sleek frame did one thing and Vee's abundance does another — and praises her aliveness, the involuntary tells (wet, flush, hard nipples) read aloud as evidence that she is landing. The praise is the engine: exposure is Vee's excitement, not her wound, so being seen and named drives the shame-arousal loop hard rather than annihilating her. The shame is that her body keeps announcing its wanting; the reframe is that he delights in exactly that. She is multi-orgasmic where Randi was wrung once; the drama is not whether she breaks and begs but a body that keeps saying yes past where she can manage it. She capitulates — but to release, not breach. Where Randi's bench created a need she had no equipment for (the wound), Vee's feeds a hunger she was always built to run.

## POV (the freshness, and the contrast)

Rendered from **inside** Vee the whole time — the inverse of the opening, which held Randi's interior at arm's length (Pace's POV, outside) until the bathroom mirror. The vantage *is* the characters: Randi opaque even on the bench, Vee transparent. This is the interior the opening deliberately withheld.

## Craft — not a repeated beat

Three levers keep it fresh, each sharpening the Randi/Vee fork rather than diluting it:
1. **POV flip** (above) — same furniture, opposite vantage.
2. **Skip the apparatus tour.** The reader knows the bench now; what cost the opening ~200 lines of discovery, disguise, and mechanism costs a glance here. Reader-memory of Randi's scene runs underneath every beat Vee does differently — that is how the sameness shows the difference, without re-rendering it.
3. **Invert the dramatic engine.** Randi's scene ran on extraction-through-denial and the imagined-judging-audience move (*"what would Brooke think"*) that broke her. That move is Randi-coded — evaluation is *Vee's* wound (Randi's domain, saved for the reveal), and Pace does not weaponize it against Vee. Vee's engine is overflow: the secretions reframe at full bloom, the tells welcomed, not wrung out.

## Function

The parallel-and-opposite transformation made flesh on one object: the same seeing that installed Randi's wound feeds Vee's appetite, because the apparatuses were built to do opposite things with it. Pays off the opening's plant and the bench's standing role as the continuously-updated proof that Pace's seeing was real and the failure was Randi's.

Spawns the SATC retelling: Vee, not knowing Randi was ever on it, describes the bench at a brunch — blooming, unashamed — and Randi receives a description of her own wound-site from the woman who bloomed where she broke, double register holding. The wound updated at maximum voltage.
