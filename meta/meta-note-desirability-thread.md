# Note — The Desirability Thread (Vee is desirable, on neutral evidence)

*Thread registry · fall → spring · coordinates the deposits that establish Vee's desirability outside Pace's and Randi's eyes*

## The problem

Randi's desirability is established *objectively* — her rank, the eyes she "built her whole adult life around being looked at for," the room. Vee's, until this thread, is attested mainly by **Pace and Randi** — the two who want to sleep with her, so **compromised witnesses**: their desire is the plot's erotic engine, so it proves the engine runs, not that she's objectively desirable. If the reader doesn't independently believe Vee is desirable, the machine (the threesome axis, the bloom) reads as authorial assertion, and the tragedy — a woman this desirable, taught since fourteen to fold it small — loses its weight. The fix: **witnesses with no stake.**

## The organizing distinction: involuntary vs. chosen

- **Involuntary desirability** — she's *not trying*, often actively hiding, and heads turn anyway. Unfalsifiable (she can't be accused of fishing), and the opposite of Randi's curated mode (un-molded vs. molded). **This is the objective gold**; weight the thread here.
- **Chosen desirability** — she decides to reveal and the room responds. Proves the *ceiling* of her effect, but because it's her doing, it can't carry the "independent of her" load. It's the **ownership pole** of her arc, not evidence.

The two poles also trace her liberation arc: Sep (Water Wings) = desirable *while hiding*; Nov (Induction) = desirable *while choosing to show*. Same constant fact, her relationship to it moving from hiding → owning.

## The craft levers

- **Women confirm > men confirm.** Men wanting her proves the fact but is self-interested; a **woman** (Cassie, and it can recur — Kayla, a grudging sister) saying it plainly is the objective seal, precisely because there's no want in it. Give the thread at least one clear-eyed female confirmation (Water Wings).
- **Unmanaged, always.** Every deposit shows desirability *happening to her while she tries to dampen it* — distinguishes her from Randi and makes the tragedy land.
- **Never vanity.** Vee POV: render through others' reactions + her deflection, never self-admiration.
- **Venue fit / no creep.** A bid must land where being approached is normal (a dance bar), never where it reads as intrusion (a woman doing laps). Wrong venue colors the whole beat creepy.

## Guards

- **Don't drop a pickup into Water Wings.** The pool's third-party reactions stay *ambient* — no one crosses the deck to her. The specific bid lives at Jitterbug.
- **Don't ask the Induction to prove the objective case.** Chosen + Pace's dress — doubly compromised for "independent of her / of Pace." Let it be the ownership pole.
- **Space and vary.** A few well-placed, different-in-kind instances — never a drumbeat of men hitting on Vee (tips to a tiresome trope, and to vain).
