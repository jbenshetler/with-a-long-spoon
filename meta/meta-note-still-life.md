# Notes — Still Life

*Companion to `meta-condensed-still-life.md`. Holds the craft intent deliberately kept off the page — the erotic engine, the forward-seeds, the guards a draft or continuity pass will need. For the surface beats, read the condensed. Renamed from "The Painting Afternoon" (git carries the history); the retelling is **Gesso** (`meta-condensed-gesso.md`).*

---

## The title (dual read)

*Still Life* — first read: the genre. He paints her as fruit; the photo is a still image; a Dutch table of abundance. Reread: a still life is an **arranged object held motionless to be looked at** — she is the plenty laid out, and the afternoon she names an *adventure* is the one where she is most composed *by him*. The **cornucopia** doubles it: the horn of plenty for the Thanksgiving table (this seats the scene the week before break) **and** her own overflow she can't contain. The retelling takes Randi's one-word line as its title — *Gesso*: the white ground primed onto a canvas to make it receptive before any paint touches it.

## The engine — a still life that won't hold still

The shame occasion here is **not** exposure (spent by now) — it's the **failure to hold the pose.** Arranged as the cornucopia, told to keep still, her body won't obey the frame: the flinch under the brush, the breath she can't level, the wet she can't stop, the small sounds. That is the fresh indignity and a clean riser on the shame staircase — not *he sees me* but *I cannot be the still object even when I want to be; my body keeps announcing itself.* **Lean into the aliveness against the "still":** the struggle to keep still, the bodily responses, the excess fluids. The cornucopia carries the overflow — abundance read as her own excess she can't hold in.

Governing register (unchanged from the brief): **fun, sexy only** — the lightest, most playful beat (heat-by-kind; earns the dark by contrast). Shadowless on purpose. Keep the fluids/overflow warm and dirty-gorgeous, never clinical, never solemn.

## Sequence: painting → photo → sex → coinage

Waxing agreed-to with noticed ease (bare canvas) — the wax itself is its own prep beat just before, *Barely Stings* (`meta-note-barely-stings.md`), which carries the fresh indifferent-witness shame and Randi's *barely stings* lie so this scene can stay pure joy. Painted as fruit — a **grapevine** up the inner arm (wrist → armpit → side of breast), **cantaloupe** at the breasts (fruit and flesh the same round shape), **cherries** at the mons; brush on skin as light sensation-play (and the first strokes she keeps breaking). **No peach here.** The peach-as-her-body image *originates* at the cabin blazon and seeds *forward* to `meta-note-peaches.md` (the July apex) — planting it in November would pre-spend that apex and dilute a clean two-beat metaphor→flesh arc (cabin: *you're a peach* → Peaches: *a peach, inside you*). Brandied peaches sit on the counter here as pure **cornucopia texture**, not a plant. The **photo** she wants — *she only likes how she looks through his eyes* — the earliest, best-disguised seed of the **sole-channel motif**; a dirty-gorgeous picture she loves, played light, never solemn. Then sex; the paint smears them and the sheets bright. Afterglow, her head on his chest, both a wreck — Vee, smiling: *"Well, that was an adventure,"* and the helpless, can't-breathe laughter. **This is where *adventure* is coined** — hers, joyful, descriptive, born in laughter at the mess, the word riding out unremarked.

## The Thanksgiving exchange

Placed in the afterglow or the dressing-after, a natural *"What about Thanksgiving?"* Two things surface, both light, neither dwelt on:

- **Sheri, named.** Pace is cooking for a friend — Sheri. He won't give her story (*that's Sheri's to tell*, held as plain decency, not a beat) — only **"She's alone."** The line does four things: warmth; respect for Sheri's autonomy (rhymes his own vault-keeping); it defuses toward *pity, not romance* (points Vee inward/safe, not toward a rival); and it's a sidelong glimpse of *his own* aloneness by proxy (he's the other one with no family table — canon: *he won't go back*, `meta-arch-sheri.md` / Cheeseburgers). Keep Sheri **offstage/reported** — her first on-page meeting is *Another Round* (~mid-March); a dinner he cooks would flatten her kinetic register.
- **The family shadow — deflect, then one fact.** Vee pushes a little on his family; his warm/present voice goes **precise and formal** (the canon stress-tell), and he gives **one flat fact only** — *I don't go home* / *we don't talk* — never the why. The **cause stays vaulted**: no bike-dragging (reserved for the spring Scar telling, `meta-note-scar-reveal.md`), no first love (reserved late summer, `meta-note-first-love.md`). Effect, never cause — progressive opening that reveals to the reader while keeping the vault shut. Consistent with, and setting up, the Cheeseburgers "he won't go back."

- **"Adventure"** — coined here, joyful, hers → first *invited* at the cabin (`meta-note-cabin-weekend.md`) → detonates at the threesome. Rides out unremarked; never named as a code.
- **Peach — deliberately NOT seeded here.** The peach-as-her-body image originates at the cabin blazon (*peach with a bite out of it*) and seeds forward to peaches.md (the July apex); peach=sex is legible cold (no setup needed), and a November plant would pre-spend the apex. The counter's brandied peaches are cornucopia texture only.
- **Sheri as the absorber → the found hair.** Naming Sheri here **arms the spring found-hair beat** (`meta-note-the-found-hair.md`): the safe woman Vee will reach for when a trace of a woman turns up in his house, so the outward question never forms. Plant here, spend in spring.
