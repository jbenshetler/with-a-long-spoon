## Register

This is the **CW-dance debrief**, a distinct SATC register: **counsel, not confession.** (The first-night telling is handled by the Fed.)

## Timing

**Friday morning — the morning after the Thursday dance.** The overnight is structurally load-bearing: Vee already knows she was wrong before she leaves the dance hall — the older woman's correction landed complete Thursday night, no ambiguity. The overnight is not processing; it's penance. She stews, morose, turning the irony over: she raged at a man for being a good man, accused him of the thing he pointedly doesn't do, performed the proprietary jealousy she'd have mocked in another woman. By Friday morning she has shifted from hot embarrassment to cold, clear *I have to fix this.* The debrief happens after that shift. One day remains before A Recognized Method (Saturday evening) — she gets the counsel and the shoes Friday, acts Saturday.

## Venue

**Randi's single.** Vee comes to Randi needing something — she shows up at the door rather than Randi arranging the meeting. This inverts the usual power geometry: Randi receives, Vee arrives. The room is private, face-to-face, Randi can read every tell. Not the velvet booth; the velvet booth is reserved for the full extraction production. The single is stripped of apparatus — just the two of them, which reads as intimacy and is also Randi in full command of her own space. **Social cost beat:** a sister seeing the unaffiliated outsider cross the sorority house threshold — especially in the distressed morning-after register — feeds the Social Price #1 vignette, which currently lacks a specific inciting incident.

## The Irony

The engine of the scene: **Vee is confessing to the woman Pace is sleeping with that she misread Pace as a womanizer.** Randi's validation of Pace is perfectly credible — she's an eyewitness — and perfectly self-serving. The truth and the lie are the same sentence. Randi gives genuinely good counsel and steers the outcome toward the configuration simultaneously. Don't name the irony; let the reader carry it.

## Randi's Counsel

**The apology structure.** Full-throated, no reservation, no hedging — the opposite of what Randi herself would do. Randi is a strategic calculator who never gives an unconditional anything; she prescribes to Vee the posture she structurally cannot occupy herself. First read: generous wisdom from the experienced friend. Reread: she's scripting the full submission that most serves the plan.

**The reframe.** *You only get that jealous about what matters. He saw how much you feel — now go show him the rest.* Takes Vee's worst moment and converts it from failure (I lost control) into signal (I'm invested). The appetite-reframe in the counsel register: Vee's embarrassing act becomes proof of depth. Warm, accurate, cold.

**Arrive with desire, not apology.** Don't show up shrinking. Show up knowing you're the woman he wants to see, and let the apology come from confidence rather than a crouch. The counsel is calibrated to Pace specifically — Randi knows he responds to the real thing, not performed contrition. She is scripting her own lover's other woman's apology from insider knowledge. Don't render this.

**The wardrobe consultation.** Randi asks what Vee has — *what are we working with.* First time Randi is thinking about Vee's bedroom, Vee's things; she's building a picture she'll use. First read: the glamorous friend helping you get your man back. Tonight the play is Vee's *own* best asset, so Randi steers from Vee's own wardrobe toward whatever shows Vee's chest — the feature she openly admired in How It's Done, the one she knows Pace has already responded to. (Not that Randi *can't* lend clothes — she can, and will; her clothes simply fit Vee *differently*, shorter and more shown, and the clothing-loan thread opens later at On Her Floor, where the borrowed skirt disproves Vee's own assumption that nothing of Randi's fits her but the shoes. Tonight the asset is Vee's, so the wardrobe is Vee's.) The advice is accurate and self-serving simultaneously.

**The shoe loan.** The thing that travels between them *cleanly* — feet fit where bodies differ, so a shoe of Randi's is just a shoe on Vee, where her clothes, lent, transform on her (shorter, tighter, more shown). That clean fit is what makes the shoe the recurring loan-object across the year and the artifact that carries into the reveal. Randi's language (established at Shoe Shopping — her hands on Vee's feet fitting shoes, physical installation). **This is the first loan in what becomes a recurring pattern across the year** — whenever Vee is going to see Pace, Randi loans shoes. She chooses from her own archive: she knows exactly what she wore to every visit with Pace, what happened, the specific details. The shoes she selects carry that private history. Vee doesn't know she's wearing a garment with Randi's Pace memories on it. Pace may register something familiar he can't name.

**Extends the physical staircase quietly:** Shoe Shopping had Randi's hands on Vee's feet; now Randi's shoes go to Pace's room on Vee's feet. The touch migrates across the body-difference gap — the gap a shoe crosses cleanly, where a borrowed garment crosses it only by fitting Vee differently. Nobody comments. On reread: Randi is present in the room, literally, when Vee makes the apology.

**Establishes the motif that culminates at the threesome** — where Randi lends Vee *fuck-me pumps* specifically chosen once Vee has confided (given the SATC closeness) that a big sexy event is coming. The history of the loan is always one-directional: Randi carries it, Vee does not.

## Vee's Addition: The Humble Pie

Not Randi's advice — Vee's own translation of the counsel into her idiom. A store-bought pie with a handmade *Humble Pie* label. The light quick thing that gets out ahead of discomfort; the self-deprecating gesture that makes the threshold crossable. Randi would probably wince at it — too cute, too much armor, the joke as cover for the naked apology underneath. But Vee can't do unconditional vulnerability without some frame. The pie lets her be Vee while doing the Randi-prescribed thing. Pace will see both: the joke and what's behind it.
