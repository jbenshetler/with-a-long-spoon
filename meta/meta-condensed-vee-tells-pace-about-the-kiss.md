# Condensed — Vee Tells Pace About the Kiss (the threesome plant)

*Scene · Unwritten · ~early February · just after On Her Floor · Vee POV*

The mixer kiss (On Her Floor) has nowhere to go: Vee can't take it to Randi (it was framed as a show for Harrison, so naming it exposes her own want), and Cassie can't hold the bodily/queer charge. So she brings it to Pace — the only door left. Processing it turns hot; they have sex; *afterward*, in the afterglow, he asks whether she's ever thought about being with a woman.

**Function — the threesome plant.** His question is the seed: deniable, real curiosity AND the move at once (tender and instrumental, never resolved). It ends on the open question — nothing agreed, nothing named. The conversion to *a threesome, with you* is hers, a later day (see chronology: *A Threesome, With You*); the fully-interactive shape is the Randi-steered spring escalation that lands at the Agreement. Full arc + mechanism in [[meta-note-threesome-reveal]] (the threesome staircase).

**Also does:**
- Motivates the still-**unannounced** Forbidden Kiss (taste-thread gate #1) — keep the coated kiss deniable; this scene gives the *"I'm not gay"* door-slam a concrete referent (the just-happened Randi kiss).
- **The two tracks collide through Pace.** Pace knows Randi intimately (they're lovers). When Vee names *Randi* as the girl she kissed, what he reveals/conceals is charged and **unstaged** — the one craft point still open.

**Placement guard:** before The Jar, so Randi's control of the Pace-channel leaks (The Jar's cold open has Randi probe *"you didn't tell Pace, did you?"* — by then he already knows).

**Tone:** warm on both reads; the cold (his plant; her handing him the safety-lever as an act of intimacy) is the reader's only. Body before mind — she names none of the machinery.
