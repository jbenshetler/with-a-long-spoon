# Condensed — First Weekend Back (Reconciliation — the lie)

*SCENE · Draft complete · first weekend of spring semester, after the long break · before {{The Reach}}*

The first step of the book's bargain — **aliveness over pride** (not yet over grounding): Vee sets down the *demand* to be named while her hope stays alive; the grounded love's death is reserved for {{Grace}} (reader-side). **The relationship's fatal flaw opens here (neither understands yet):** she offers her body, the channel that still works, to get past the door without the conversation she can't have — a detour around the word, not a toll, done once and freely, Pace demanding nothing and reading no transaction. Genuine desire AND avoidance at once (the real-AND-instrumental register), her agency intact. The mark is self-administered: she learns the relationship can run through the body with the word never coming, and downstream the erotic turns **anesthetic** — she escalates intensity to distract from what's missing, which later carries her toward the threesome (`meta-note-threesome-reveal.md`). After the Christmas fight they came apart and she went flat over the break. The reconciliation day is now **three beats in one day** (~Fri Jan 22 — the compression carrying her urgency): **The Stranger** (door one, morning), **{{My Friend Randi}}** (the pivot, afternoon at Randi's single — its own note, SATC-track), **Nothing Underneath** (door two, night); the honest daylight reach fails, the wordless night offering works. **Door one:** she still wants to be *named* and gets his cold (the stress-tell — fear, not a verdict; not a second fight, the question stays open all year); she reaches past *Pace* to **"Peter"** and it fails against the cold; she leaves crying. **{{My Friend Randi}} (the pivot):** wrecked from the morning, Vee goes to Randi (as after the CW Dance), vents the silence and the **Sheri fear she won't put to Pace**, and Randi coaches her — **the nothing-under-the-coat appeal is Randi's idea**; the confidante track produces the act that saves the relationship (she makes with words what Pace makes with his body). The internal setting-down stays unnarrated; big long hug (flicker too-long) + Randi's scent taken in like Pace's (reader-side only) + the usual settled kiss; the Sheri fear soothed emotionally, the experiential dissolve reserved for Another Round. Craft: `meta-note-my-friend-randi.md`. **Door two (`nothing-underneath.md`):** she *puts herself together* — long coat, heels, **nothing underneath** — and the approach is now the scene's engine, run on **agency, not shame**: at the mirror she takes the body she's hidden since fourteen and turns it to its purpose, works herself to the edge and **denies herself the finish, saving it for him** (protects {{The Reach}}); a reckless drive with a second self-touch and a police-cruiser false alarm that leaves her denied twice and wound tighter; the locked door and the knock, everything staked on one card. The fresh shame is **premeditation** (wanting on display as a thing she chose; one riser below the Cabin's watched exposure), rendered as **bravery and power**, not the old involuntary tell. Pace says one distancing line; she doesn't reach for Peter (the demand set down, not the hope) and answers wordless by **opening the coat** (Randi's costume). What moves Pace is her **willing self-transgression** (his appetite) AND that she came (genuine), no seam. Then the body-over-word inversion made literal: **he takes her with his mouth on the cold doorstep** where his words failed that morning (seeding his relish → {{Forbidden Kiss}}), then a starved standing-to-couch fuck (never making the bed) at **reunion-ferocity** inside already-crossed acts (no new threshold; spring risers reserved). **Afterglow = First Night, shadowed:** the tender come-down; **her name restored** (*"make an entrance, Vee"* — the morning's stranger-treatment undone, drawing the tear); midnight scratch pancakes (the fall-for-Pace warmth); and the cloud as **one** submerged beat — his voice cracking on *"I missed you so much"* where the word would be, she too full to reach for it (*demand dead, hope alive*; never narrated). A **scene coda**: Sunday she walks into the dorm in her coat and **his sweats** (clothing-proxy) and the heels, to Cassie's dry *"Hmm."* **Pace gives no answer here** (the vow holds; the Cabin **key** is his eventual soothe-not-close, and the cabin — not this scene — is where the grounded love gets its reader-side death; protect both). **Taste:** Vee's self-taste carries nothing ({{The Reach}} is the next gate); Pace's relish for her taste is seeded at the doorstep oral (the setup for {{Forbidden Kiss}}). **Coda:** the **bomber jacket and broadcloth among friends without being asked** — the proprietary feeling she hasn't earned the right to, the claim worn while still unnamed.
