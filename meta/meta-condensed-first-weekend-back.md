# Condensed — First Weekend Back (Reconciliation — the lie)

*SCENE · Unwritten · first weekend of spring semester, after the long break · before The Reach*

The first step of the book's bargain — **aliveness over pride** (not yet over grounding): Vee sets down the *demand* to be named while her hope stays alive; the grounded love's death is reserved for the Cabin Weekend (reader-side). **The relationship's fatal flaw opens here (neither understands yet):** the transgression is under duress — her flatness, which Pace *cultivated*, though he never asked; his consent-architecture checks only for *force*, so her willing initiation reads to him as pure consent while it's the camouflage. Safety converts from unconditional ground to a conditional thing she keeps earning; the lost ground later drives the threesome (`meta-note-threesome-reveal.md`). After the Christmas fight they came apart and she went flat over the break. The reconciliation day is now **three beats in one day** (~Fri Jan 22 — the compression carrying her urgency): **Back In Town** (door one, morning), **My Friend Randi** (the pivot, afternoon at Randi's single — its own note, SATC-track), **Nothing Underneath** (door two, night); the honest daylight reach fails, the wordless night offering works. **Door one:** she still wants to be *named* and gets his cold (the stress-tell — fear, not a verdict; not a second fight, the question stays open all year); she reaches past *Pace* to **"Peter"** and it fails against the cold; she leaves crying. **My Friend Randi (the pivot):** wrecked from the morning, Vee goes to Randi (as after the CW Dance), vents the silence and the **Sheri fear she won't put to Pace**, and Randi coaches her — **the nothing-under-the-coat appeal is Randi's idea**; the confidante track produces the act that saves the relationship (she makes with words what Pace makes with his body). The internal setting-down stays unnarrated; big long hug (flicker too-long) + Randi's scent taken in like Pace's (reader-side only) + the usual settled kiss; the Sheri fear soothed emotionally, the experiential dissolve reserved for Another Round. Craft: `meta-note-my-friend-randi.md`. **Door two:** she's *put herself together* — long coat, heels, **nothing underneath**; her breath steaming the only weather note; Pace says one distancing line; she doesn't reach for Peter (the demand set down, not the hope) and answers wordless by **opening the coat** (the costume Randi's counsel). The coat = vulnerability, literally and emotionally naked; the fresh shame is **premeditation** (wanting on display as a thing she chose — not caught, not involuntary; one riser below the Cabin's watched exposure). What moves Pace is her **willing self-transgression** (his appetite) AND that she came (genuine), no seam — never a reward for submission, never strategy. The sex runs at **reunion-ferocity** inside already-crossed acts (no new threshold — the spring risers are reserved); flat-to-alive **on the body**, so the trade keeps her dignity. **Afterglow = First Night with an unresolved cloud:** snuggle, food, wine, genuinely warm on first read; the cloud is **one** beat — Pace's loving **non-answer** (the porch grammar, warmth routed around the word) that only that morning drove her away and that she now *lets pass* — never narrated. **Pace gives no answer here** (the vow holds; the Cabin **key** is his eventual soothe-not-close, and the cabin — not this scene — is where the grounded love gets its reader-side death; protect both). **Taste: carry nothing** (The Reach is the next gate). **Coda:** the **bomber jacket and broadcloth among friends without being asked** — the proprietary feeling she hasn't earned the right to, the claim worn while still unnamed.
