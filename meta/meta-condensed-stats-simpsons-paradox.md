# Condensed — Simpson's Paradox (Stats Class)

*SCENE · Draft complete (`scenes/stats-simpsons-paradox.md`) · morning after Dress Pickup · stats class day (MWF) · Vee POV*

Absorbs the old standalone "Vee Shows Cassie the Dress." The dress-cluster's confidante beat, relocated to the statistics lecture, where Vee sits between both girls (Cassie | Vee | Randi). She comes in late and glowing off the overnight at Pace's; the finished gown is home at the dorm, zipped in its bag, shown to no one.

## What it does

The dress is old news to both girls (the fitting tellings — `turned-up.md` and How It's Done), so **neither re-reveals it.** The new thing is the completed, transformed Vee, and the fork is in how each friend meets her:

- **Cassie watches Vee, not the dress.** She does not care about dresses; she cares about what the dress did to her friend. Forty seconds of the lecture pass with her attention never leaving Vee. "Was it a good night?" / "Yes. Oh, yes." Something eases in her; she goes back to her notes, reassured.
- **Randi hears the other thing** in that second *yes* ("You lucky slut"), then draws the shoe-gap out of Vee and binds it with a favor ("A dress like that deserves the right shoes… How's Saturday"). The steering stays invisible: Vee surfaces the lack, Randi offers the fix, Vee is grateful. Sets up Shoe Shopping (where the bag comes out for Randi).

Both are glad for Vee; the orientations differ (the person vs. the deployment). The "Oh, yes" is the fork in one word — same data, two readings.

## The stats mirror

**Simpson's paradox**, bookended (Vee catches the open and the close, loses the middle to the fork). Rendered as the general principle, not just the box score: a thing can run one way across every short stretch and the opposite way across the whole, and the total hands you the reverse of the truth without lying about a number. The reread engine (short-term warmth, long-term trap) in neutral baseball terms; Vee transcribes it, connects it to nothing. Stats-mirror progression: latent variables / residuals (`see-you-later`) → Simpson's here. The bookend (open + close) is the reusable device. The mirror *function* is carried all year, but the **classroom vehicle turns over with the semester**: fall stats (the aggregate that lies) hands off to the **spring anthropology class** (exchange, taboo, ritual) — see `meta-note-anthro.md`.

## On reread

The aggregate (a man lovingly dressing his girl, two friends sharing her joy) reverses once the hidden grouping (Randi's operation) is seen — the thing the Simpson's drone is quietly about.
