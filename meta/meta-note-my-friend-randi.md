# Note — My Friend Randi (the pivot of the reconciliation day)

*Scene · Architecture complete, prose not drafted · ~Fri Jan 22, afternoon · Randi's single · Vee POV · SATC/confidante track · the middle beat of the reconciliation day (Back In Town → **My Friend Randi** → Nothing Underneath). Umbrella for the day: `meta-note-first-weekend-back.md`. Track doc: `meta-plan-satc-tracks.md`.*

The title **is** the irony: the real other woman, called *friend* — the one Vee runs to, the one who sends her back. Sharpen it, never wink at it.

---

## What this scene is for

Three jobs, all warm on the first read:

1. **The confidante track produces the reconciliation.** First Weekend Back's door two ("nothing under the coat") used to be Vee's own move. Now it is **Randi's counsel** — *stop reaching for the word; show him what you are.* The SATC engine stops merely commenting on the relationship and starts **making the act that saves it**: she produces with words what Pace produces with his body. The most consequential steering the confidante track ever does.
2. **The un-askable question finds its address.** Vee does **not** ask Pace who he was with over the break — she has no name, no standing, no right. So the question goes to Randi instead. That is the confidante track's core mechanism: Randi is the repository for everything Vee can't say to Pace. She can keep it that way — steer Vee *away* from the direct path ("don't be the girl who interrogates him"), so the questions keep flowing to her and never to him. Deniable, loving, and it closes the one road that would resolve things cleanly.
3. **The SATC-register blonde-irony completes.** Vee brings her terror of the **wrong** other woman (innocent Sheri) to the **real** one (Randi), who soothes it — and then sends her back into the unnamed configuration. The NYE cross-cut (`meta-note-vee-old-acquaintances.md`), now live and verbal: the rival comforts her rival's fear and coaches her rival's rescue.

## The counsel IS the plan's thesis

*Stop needing the word — give him the body.* Spoken as the boldest, most loving girlfriend advice; on reread it is the bargain itself — the exact trade the whole book runs on (aliveness/appetite over the name), voiced by the architect of it. Randi coaching Vee to speak in body instead of demanding the word is her **grooming the instrument** for what the body is being cultivated toward (the threesome), dressed as friendship. Deliver it with zero knowingness; the cold is reread-only.

## The Sheri-vent — soothed, not solved

- Randi almost certainly **hasn't met Sheri** (different worlds). So she cannot defuse the fear with the fact (*she's a lesbian*) — she soothes it **emotionally**, pure management, no information. Better: it keeps the worry **live** (balmed, not resolved), so it reactivates on Sheri-proximity and dissolves **experientially at Another Round**. Two-track handling of one anxiety — verbal here, bodily there.
- The reframe is **appetite-in-velvet** (`meta-plan-satc-tracks.md`): Vee's fear received as proof of caring, installed as a permanent condition of loving Pace — the more she loves, the more she'll worry, the more she'll need Randi's reassurance. Warm on the surface; a dependency Randi controls, on reread.

## The physical — a new vector, not a new kiss-rung

The goodbye-kiss staircase does **not** escalate here: the **usual settled kiss** closes the scene, by now unremarkable ("only what they do"). What advances is a **different vector**:

- **The hug.** Big and long, fully covered by *best friends who badly missed each other* (only the midnight text bridged the break). Its charge is **duration only** — a flicker of too-long, the beat she holds past the end, the not-wanting-to-let-go. Rendered by the holding, **never** by Vee thinking the hug is different. (Same rule as the goodbye kiss: specificity and duration, never cognition.)
- **The scent — the load-bearing beat.** Inside the hug, Vee **breathes Randi in deeply, the way she breathes Pace in.** This runs Randi through the *same involuntary sensory channel* as Pace — the His-Shirt/animal-evidence register where the body registers wanting before the mind consents. The shame-engine, firing on the Randi track: her body confesses in an embrace she can wholly explain. Two seductions, one nervous system.
  - **Rail (do not break):** the Pace parallel stays **entirely reader-side.** On the page she pulls in a breath, deep, and doesn't want to stop — **that is all.** She must never think *I'm breathing her in the way I breathe him in*; the moment she cognizes it, it's forensic and the device dies. "(Like she does Pace's)" is note-intent and reader-inference — the reader who has watched the Pace scent beats supplies it. Body before mind, never named.
  - **Pre-seed:** she takes the scent in **unnamed** here; she asks its name later (the perfume-name gate, placement open through March — `meta-plan-satc-tracks.md`). This is where the wanting-to-know is planted.
  - On the staircase this is a genuine step — arguably more intimate than a kiss-rung precisely because it's involuntary and unspeakable — well-placed for the late-January "latent → palpable" stretch of Vee's pull toward Randi.

## Format & POV

- **Format-break, not a brunch.** A reunion wants intimacy: **Randi's single.** Breaks the brunch monopoly (the track wants this) and suits a confidence over a public seated apparatus.
- **No bathroom beat.** The shame here is **relational** (jealousy, not-enough-ness), not erotic/transgressive — the shame-arousal mechanism does not fire. This is a warmth/refuge scene, not an apparatus scene.
- **Vee POV; Randi opaque.** No interior crack. Randi's single is the **setting**, not an aperture — her interior stays locked to the containers rule (flag 15; the next aperture is Spring Inversion, mid-Feb). The reader infers everything from Vee's reading of Randi's warmth and belief in her; whether Randi is moved or filing is never given.
- **Double register — every line passes both tests, no word changing.** Worked example — Vee: *"I kept picturing him with her the whole break."* Randi, warm: *"Of course you did — that's how you know he's yours to lose."* First read: a friend normalizing heartbreak. Reread: she installs the worry as permanent, the dependency deepening. Aim every line there.

## Guards

- **Earn the dark by being light.** A genuinely warm reunion — two people who missed each other, one falling apart, the other catching her. Cut any signal that Randi is sinister or that the advice is anything but love. Suspicion kills the scene; the cold is pattern-and-reread only.
- **Preserve Vee's agency.** Randi supplies the **method** (the coat); the **surrender** — actually setting the word down and going — stays **Vee's**, so Nothing Underneath still reads as her brave choice, not Randi's puppetry. Randi tips the scale; Vee steps off it.
- **Keep the internal setting-down unnarrated.** The counsel is scene'd; Vee's renunciation of the demand is not. She never thinks *I'll stop needing to be named* — body before mind, the reader infers the trade.
- **Don't pre-spend Another Round.** Randi soothes the Sheri fear without the fact; the experiential dissolve (the proprietary brace, `meta-note-another-round.md`) belongs there.
- **The CW-Dance rhyme.** Vee running to Randi in crisis is established behavior (the CW-Dance debrief). Lean on it lightly — every Pace-crisis drives her deeper into Randi's orbit; that pattern *is* the cold.

## Threads & seeds
