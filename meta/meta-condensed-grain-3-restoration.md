# Condensed — Grain #3 — The Restoration (The Loud One)

*VIGNETTE (Vee POV, mirror-time) · Architecture complete, prose not drafted · April, after stakes/trust are high · slot TBD, deeper in*

Vee has depleted the form to ~20. Randi returns ~4 (20 → 24) — the form goes **up**. In Vee's model of the world this is not improbable but impossible: the drawer is hers, he never touches it (the gift's whole grammar, founded at {{The Top Drawer}}), and she never re-threads the form. Because she's been half-watching since the First Flicker and the base is low, +4 is unmissable. Direction beats magnitude: a small change in the impossible direction is louder than a large one in the plausible direction.

**The return itself is off-page and unmarked, like the first visit** — no POV, no staging, no opportunity beat. Vee's catch is the rendered beat: mirror-time, alone, mid-ritual. **The reader does the same arithmetic Vee does, with the same data.** No count is ever explicit on the page; the numbers (50/38/32/20/24) are a working model for the shape, carried as her vague running sense.

## The landing — wonder, checked, vacuum, self-doubt

The wonder about Pace arrives — *did he notice mine running low? did he quietly—* — a deed-declaration in the exact channel she's been auditing since {{Believe Me}}, and it would mean something so warm she can feel the pull of it. And it fails on her own accurate knowledge: the drawer is hers; he gave the space and stepped back; not touching it is the *point* of it. The sweetness of the idea flickers and dies — killed not by fear but by her correct reading of him and of the arrangement. **Settling on Pace would reduce uncertainty; nothing is allowed to.** No candidate story remains — someone-else never forms (following anomalies is what she's been trained out of) — so the anomaly has no narrative to absorb it, and the self absorbs it: *I must be wrong about what was there.* Nothing resolves. What installs is the operational lesson: **drop the anomaly, don't follow it.**

Her accuracy produces her self-doubt — the book's signature move: right about Pace, right about the count, and the only exit left is *then I must be wrong about myself.*

## The target is epistemic, never evidentiary

Her case for his love is an inference from abundant physical observation — the dress that fits because his hands measured her, the warm house, the meals, the form in her color, the drawer itself. The grains attack the instrument, not the signal: after this beat, every reading carries a margin of error, and her case is built entirely of readings. This is why the missing words ache more in spring, not less — a spoken declaration is the one piece of evidence that wouldn't route through her instrument; the thing he won't give is the only thing that would be gaslight-proof, and she can't know that's why the want of it has changed weight. The actions-vs-words grit (the cohabitation question: lived reality full, named status absent) was real and dignified before any grain landed; the gaslight removes the ground she stood on while living with it.

## Two unaskable questions, one room

*Do you love me* and *did the form go up* — both perceptions accurate, both unaskable (asking either would break the register it lives in), each doubt teaching the other: if I can be wrong about a drawer, how do I trust what I think I see in him? The bathroom is full of his provision — the warm house, his towel, her drawer, her color — and she stands in it learning to distrust her reading of evidence. The room itself is the argument.

## The configuration is the agent

Randi's sabotage and Pace's wordlessness cooperate without coordination: his refusal of the words is what makes the deed-channel load-bearing enough to be worth attacking; her noise lands in the channel he transmits on. Neither monitors the register. Nobody planned this harm; the structure produced it.

## Function and calibration

Trains Vee to override her own correct readings — the campaign's true objective: disabling her clue-detector so later, bigger grains get filed under *I'm probably imagining it.* {{Grain #2}}'s flattery filing protects from the other side ("Randi wears scrunchies like mine" is already shelved under friendship, so it never combines with the arithmetic). The stats resonance — a noisy-data inference problem handed to a statistics student — stays unnamed. Vee stays the settled one: a stone in the shoe, not a spiral; the beat's register is quietly frightening, not destabilized.
