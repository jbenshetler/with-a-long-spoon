*SCENE · Architecture complete, prose not drafted · days after Barely Stings, after Forbidden Kiss*

**Playful going in, transgressive at the turn** (heat-by-kind; earns the dark by opening light — In His Hands now carries the fall warmth). Sequence: **painting → the runaway → photo → sex.** Bare canvas from the wax just before ([[meta-condensed-barely-stings]]); painted as fruit — grapevine (wrist → armpit → side of breast), cantaloupe (breasts), cherries (mons); he traces each route with his mouth, then the cool brush, and **feeds her the fruit he paints her as** (grape, then melon) so the build keeps breaking. **No peach here** — originates at the cabin blazon, seeds forward to peaches.md (the July apex); a plant now pre-spends it. A **still life that won't hold still** — arranged as the **cornucopia** (she *is* the plenty, lush, abundant, overflowing), the shame is the *failure to keep the pose*: can't-keep-still, bodily responses, and the overflow she can't contain, which he gathers and paints back onto her. The **runaway** is the mons: **painted in her own fluids** — the arousal/shame crest, her wanting made medium. **Taste thread, held:** she tastes the cherry (clean fruit) and, watching his relish, *wonders* about her own taste — no ask (→ Whiskey and Wine), no contact (→ The Almost), no taste (→ First Taste). The **photo** she wants — of her nude, waxed, painted, transformed body, to hand to Pace and show Randi at Gesso — the **escalation** of the plain nude from *In His Hands* (which holds the earliest sole-channel seed); she loves it because it's how he sees her. Then sex; the paint smears them and the sheets bright. Detail + forward-seeds: [[meta-note-still-life]].
