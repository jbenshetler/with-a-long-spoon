# Condensed — Still Life

*SCENE · Architecture complete, prose not drafted · ~mid-November, the week before Thanksgiving break*

**Fun, sexy only** — the lightest, most playful beat (heat-by-kind; earns the dark by contrast). Sequence: **painting → photo → sex → coinage.** Waxing agreed-to with noticed ease (bare canvas; the wax itself is its own prep beat → [[meta-condensed-barely-stings]]); painted as fruit — grapevine (wrist → armpit → side of breast), cantaloupe (breasts), cherries (mons); brush on skin as light sensation-play. **No peach here** — the peach-as-body image originates at the cabin blazon and seeds *forward* to peaches.md (the July apex); planting it now would pre-spend that apex. Counter peaches are cornucopia texture, not a plant. A **still life that won't hold still** — arranged as the cornucopia (horn-of-plenty; she is the plenty), the shame is the *failure to keep the pose*: can't-keep-still, bodily responses, excess fluids. The **photo** she wants — *she only likes how she looks through his eyes* — the earliest, best-disguised seed of the **sole-channel motif** (→ the cabin's "he becomes her eyes" → the bench → the threesome kiss); played **light**, a dirty-gorgeous picture she loves, never solemn. Then sex; the paint smears them and the sheets bright. Afterglow, her head on his chest, both a wreck — Vee, smiling: *"Well, that was an adventure,"* and the helpless, can't-breathe laughter. **This is where *adventure* is coined** — hers, joyful, descriptive, born in laughter at the mess, the word riding out unremarked (origin of the arc → first *invite* at the cabin → detonation at the threesome; see meta-note-cabin-weekend.md). **Thanksgiving exchange:** *"What about Thanksgiving?"* — he's cooking for Sheri (*"She's alone"*; Sheri offstage/reported, the absorber planted for the spring found-hair); Vee pushes on his family and gets the shadow — deflect, then one flat fact (*I don't go home*), the cause vaulted (no scar, no first love). Shadowless on purpose: the most self-authored, joyful moment becomes the frame that later walks her in blind. Detail + forward-seeds: [[meta-note-still-life]].
