## Accepted: the "package" line is retell bravado, not a sourced beat

Vee's *"I could feel his — his package. Right here. When he was pinning the back seam"* is deliberate and stays as written. It plays as her clinical-comic exaggeration to Cassie — the same diagram-pointing bit as the grinding confession — not as a claim the reader needs corroborated.

The grinding **is** anchored in {{A Round}} (she bends into him at the back seam; presses her breast to his cheek on the third pass). Her feeling his erection is **not** rendered there and is not meant to be — {{A Round}} stays in close-third on Vee and doesn't stage it. This is an intentional gap: the retell embellishes. Do not "fix" it by anchoring the sensation in {{A Round}} or by cutting it from the retell.
