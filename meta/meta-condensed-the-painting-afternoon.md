# Condensed — The Painting Afternoon

*SCENE · Unwritten · late November, before Thanksgiving*

**Fun, sexy only** — the lightest, most playful beat (heat-by-kind; earns the dark by contrast). Sequence: **painting → photo → sex → coinage.** Waxing agreed-to with noticed ease (bare canvas); painted as fruit (peaches callback — seeds the cabin blazon's *peach with a bite out of it*); brush on skin as light sensation-play. The **photo** she wants — *she only likes how she looks through his eyes* — the earliest, best-disguised seed of the **sole-channel motif** (→ the cabin's "he becomes her eyes" → the bench → the threesome kiss); played **light**, a dirty-gorgeous picture she loves, never solemn. Then sex; the paint smears them and the sheets bright. Afterglow, her head on his chest, both a wreck — Vee, smiling: *"Well, that was an adventure,"* and the helpless, can't-breathe laughter. **This is where *adventure* is coined** — hers, joyful, descriptive, born in laughter at the mess, the word riding out unremarked (origin of the arc → first *invite* at the cabin → detonation at the threesome; see meta-note-cabin-weekend.md). Shadowless on purpose: the most self-authored, joyful moment becomes the frame that later walks her in blind.
