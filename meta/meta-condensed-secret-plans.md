# Condensed — Secret Plans (The Princess / Vee's Adventure)

*Diptych · Unwritten · ~late March–early April · replaces Nipple Clamps · companion: `meta-note-secret-plans.md`*

**The first scene Vee authors.** She invites *him* — the "adventure" word turned bidirectional at last (she coined it at the Painting Afternoon; Pace first pointed it forward at the cabin; now she uses it to invite). Her one act of erotic authorship is to commission her own capture: she asks to play a **space princess** chased, caught, stripped, and "interrogated" by a galactic overlord for the **secret plans** she won't give up. She designs the *wish*; Pace engineers the *execution*. First read: the relationship's most playful peak, a brave fun thing she's finally confident enough to ask for. Reread: the first time she holds the pen she writes herself a cage — her authorship and the machinery that runs her are the same act. The playfulness is the point — earn the dark by being light; the cold is reread-only.

**Panel 1 — The Ask.** A quiet domestic beat; she hands Pace the *premise*, not a script — shy-brave, native voice, small words (the persona is a cover she can want behind, not witty banter she performs). She names the chase / capture / strip / "torture" and leaves the **torture unspecified** — *you decide* — and **the plans she'll never reveal** as the only rule. She doesn't run it; she asks for it.

**[EVENT] Pace Builds (off-page, never rendered).** Making-as-love (rhymes the dress, the key box): he crafts a **princess costume that barely covers her at rest and gives her away in motion**, seamed with **few stitches so it tears off a piece at a time**. The sci-fi frame licenses **"alien tech" normally outside his register** — a **vibrator** and a **nipple corrector** (suction run in cycles/pulses, pleasure or light pain). The license is diegetic; structurally the devices fold *into* his signature — another way for him to own *when* relief arrives (deny-the-grind, sci-fi-skinned). Off-page protects the surprise for Vee and reader both.

**Panel 2 — The Princess.** Outdoor chase on his private land, a warm afternoon (full daylight — the daylight-hinge thread). He commits to the bit but starts **stiff**, warming as she lights up — the inversion made flesh: she's fluent in the fiction, he catches up because *she's lit by it*. **Early near-escape** — she thinks she's getting away, eases up, a disappointment she can't account for (rendered in the body, never named); that easing is the tell, and the **surge** at the end reveals he was only toying — her flight was always on his leash. Costume torn loose in the chase (exposure starts mid-flight); a distracting **swat or two** breaks her stride and lets him gain. Capture → interrogation: **device alternated with hands**, his attention the center of gravity even mid-machine. Two synchronized staircases — **the costume torn piece-by-piece** and **the persona eroded by the recurring failed grab** (she keeps reaching back for "the defiant princess" each time her body confesses, and each grab fails harder). **Symmetrical dissolve:** her princess burns off into pure wanting Vee; his stiff overlord burns off into real Pace — both frames cook away and leave the dynamic they always are. **Fresh indignity:** caught wanting it *while performing resistance* — the gap between her scripted "you'll get nothing from me" and her body's immediate yes. **Afterglow** (tender-domestic exhale): Pace teases *"So — what were the secret plans?"* → *"There were never any plans."* Funny on the surface; quietly exact underneath — the interrogation's only true extraction was her *yes*. Never explained.
