## What this scene is for

The book keeps its single best piece of Pace-decency — the disowning and his response — locked in author-knowledge, where no reader ever reaches it. Every other Pace-sympathy channel (his POV scenes, the rides, the bike-as-repair) is either about the D-role or is reader-side-only. This scene is the fix: it puts his goodness **on the page through a witness**, so the reader can fall for him as hard as Vee does — which is what makes the dark turn land. He never opens up; his goodness is *testified,* not confessed.

## The canon revision this scene makes

`meta-arch-sheri.md` held the disowning as author-knowledge, "never dramatized, never a scene," and `meta-note-sheri-first-ride.md` locked the Sheri vignettes to **Pace POV**. This scene amends both, narrowly (edits recorded in those home docs):

- The disowning **surfaces on the page exactly once**, here, in **Sheri's own dry register** — and nowhere else.
- **Vee POV** (the first Vee-POV Sheri scene): Vee meets Sheri, Pace is mostly off fetching drinks, so there's no Pace-leak to capture — the frame is Vee *receiving* his goodness from a witness.

## How to render the backstory without breaking Sheri

- **Her register, not the novel's.** Dry, fierce, working/country idiom, **zero self-pity.** The tears were *then,* recounted flat now. She would not dramatize her own pain — that's not who she is.
- **She needles even while she testifies.** She punctures Pace's intellectualism (the patent-lecture he'd give an empty room) so the warmth never goes saccharine — affection rendered as deflation.
- **Weight on Pace, not on Sheri.** Her story is the vehicle; his constancy is the cargo. Render his *specific* restraint — that he didn't say *they'll come around,* because it would insult the size of what she lost — not generic niceness. **Bacon** cheeseburgers, after a ride.
- **Not a confessional.** Barroom talk in his absences, kept kinetic by the drinking and the drink-runs, not a static heart-to-heart. (The side-by-side-movement format guard in `meta-arch-sheri.md` is about *Pace's* leak; it isn't operative here, since Pace isn't the one talking.)

## The two parts escalate

Part one is *passive* decency (the bad thing he didn't do — drunk, handsy); part two is *active* loyalty (the hard good thing he did — stayed). Rising. The drink-runs are the natural break.

## Told, then shown

Sheri narrates the man who takes care of people; then the girls get drunk and he, sober, drives them home — the testimony enacted in real time, the same night. Lean on this; it's the spine. Vee watches the story become demonstration.

## Vee's beat — the proprietary brace, then warmth

Use her established proprietary jealousy (the CW Dance): she walks in braced at how easy he is with this woman, and the brace dissolves when Sheri's a lesbian lands — not a plot point, just fact, the rival-channel switched off. What's left is warmth and, underneath, the ache: the fullest case for his love yet, and still no word from him.

## Dark-on-reread layer (do not point at it)

The decency is **real** — render it pure-warm, no sinister note anywhere in the scene. The cold is structural and felt only later: the realer his constancy, the deeper Vee binds into the bargain, and the louder the *I love you* he still won't say. The man who stayed for Sheri is the man deceiving Vee into consent; the scene must never hint it. Earn the dark by being light.

## Guards

- **Protect the Cassandra asymmetry.** Sheri does not diagnose Randi or the configuration. If Randi comes up at all, one glancing ping (*"she's a lot"*) and no more; easiest to keep Randi out of frame. (`meta-arch-sheri.md`: Pace-specific radar, not a diagnostic instrument.)
- **Sheri kinetic, never fragile** — the smallness is motion; no frailty, no eating read.
- **Sheri is secondary** — rendered through effect and function; don't over-feed her interiority.
- **Pace's virtues are real, not cynical** — his ease with Sheri and his constancy are genuinely warm first.
- **Continuity to confirm at draft:** Sheri's lesbian orientation and the met-dancing origin are canon (`meta-arch-sheri.md`); this is Vee and Sheri's first meeting on the page (no prior Vee–Sheri scene).
