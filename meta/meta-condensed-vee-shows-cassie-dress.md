# Condensed — Vee Shows Cassie the Dress

*SCENE · Unwritten · same day as scene 15d or the following day*

Vee shows Cassie the finished dress — proud, happy, with just a hint of darkness on the horizon. The drawer-thinness sits in her too formless to name; scene is mostly pure delight with the faintest shadow the reader feels and Vee doesn't. Deliberate contrast with the showing-to-Randi register at shoe-shopping (16).

## Key Structural Notes

This is scene 15e in the dress cluster, the final dress entry. It pairs structurally with 15a (Vee Tells Cassie About the Fitting), both rendered in the Cassandra-track register. The contrast is deliberate and load-bearing: where 15a was all warmth and being-seen-at-her-most-ashamed, 15e is pure delight on the surface with the scrunchie-drawer awareness sitting underneath as formless shadow — the reader feels what Vee doesn't yet name. This scene directly precedes the shoe-shopping scene (16), where the showing-to-Randi contrast will be fully articulated (Vee delighted, Randi running appraisal-as-interest sadism underneath, and the reader holding what Randi did to the drawer).
