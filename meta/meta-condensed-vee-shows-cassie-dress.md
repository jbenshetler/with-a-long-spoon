# Condensed — Vee Shows Cassie the Dress

*SCENE · Unwritten · same day as Dress Pickup or the following day*

Pure delight, nothing underneath. The expansive, social, warm scene of the dress cluster — Dress Pickup stays lean and private (want, initiation); this one spends the emotion: showing Cassie, the pride, the dress as an object of pure joy between friends. Cassandra-track register: genuine mutuality, nothing performed.

## Structural work

- Pairs with **Vee Tells Cassie About the Fitting**: experience (dress unfinished) → object (finished) across the pair. The pair's unbroken warmth makes the later cooling-off scenes (Cassie & Vee — Cooling-Off; Cassie — Spring) land harder.
- Deliberate, load-bearing contrast with the **showing-to-Randi register at Shoe Shopping**: delight received as delight vs. delight received as appraisal-as-interest. The reader holds the contrast; neither scene names it.
- On reread: the friendship Vee displaced — the warmth that was always available without a price.
